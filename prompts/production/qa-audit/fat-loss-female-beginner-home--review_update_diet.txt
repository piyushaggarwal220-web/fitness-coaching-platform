**MEAL 1 — BREAKFAST (8:30 AM)**

**Option A: Protein Oats Bowl**
- Rolled oats: 40g (raw)
- Milk (toned): 200ml
- Protein powder (vanilla): 1 scoop (25g)
- Almonds (chopped): 8–10 pieces
- Banana: ½ medium
- Cinnamon: pinch

**Option B: Moong Dal Cheela (2 medium)**
- Moong dal batter: 60g (soaked, ground)
- Onion, tomato, coriander: 30g mixed
- Paneer (grated, mixed in): 40g
- Cooked in 1 tsp ghee
- Side: Mint chutney (no sugar)

**Option C: Idli Plate**
- Idli: 3 medium
- Sambar: 1 bowl (150ml, lentil-heavy)
- Coconut chutney: 2 tbsp
- Boiled egg whites: 3 (or 30g roasted chana if preferred)

---

**MEAL 2 — LUNCH (1:30 PM)**

**Base Template (Daily):**
- Rice (cooked): 1 cup (150g) OR 2 medium rotis
- Dal (any): 1 bowl (200ml) — toor, moong, masoor
- Sabzi (dry): 1 bowl — bhindi, beans, lauki, capsicum, etc.
- Paneer bhurji OR curd: 80g paneer OR 150g curd
- Salad (raw): Unlimited — cucumber, tomato, carrot, onion, lemon
- Cooking oil: 1.5 tsp total for dal + sabzi

**Pro Tip:** Eat salad FIRST, then dal, then sabzi, then rice/roti. Slows eating, increases fullness.

---

**MEAL 3 — EVENING SNACK (6:30 PM)**

**Option A: Tea + Protein Snack**
- Tea (with 100ml toned milk, no sugar or 1 stevia): 1 cup
- Roasted chana: 40g
- Carrot sticks: 1 medium carrot

**Option B: Sprouts Chaat**
- Mixed sprouts (moong/chana): 80g boiled
- Onion, tomato, coriander, lemon, chaat masala
- Roasted peanuts: 1 tbsp

**Option C: Paneer Tikka (Homemade)**
- Paneer cubes: 60g
- Marinated in curd (50g) + spices, grilled/air-fried
- Served with mint chutney

**MEAL 4 — DINNER (9:00 PM)**

**Option A: Roti + Paneer + Sabzi**
- Roti (whole wheat): 2 medium

- Sabzi (any): 1 bowl
- Salad: Unlimited

**Option B: Curd Rice Bowl (Comfort Meal)**

**Option C: Dal Khichdi + Raita**

- Ghee: 1 tsp on top
