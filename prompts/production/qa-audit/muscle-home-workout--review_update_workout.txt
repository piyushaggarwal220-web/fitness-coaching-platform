**Upper/Lower Split – 4 Days per Week**

You'll now train with an upper/lower split hitting two body regions per session. Each workout remains ~50 minutes. This structure gives muscles more frequency and recovery while keeping session length manageable.

**Day 1 – Upper Body (Push Focus)**
1. Dumbbell Bench Press – 4 sets x 10 reps
2. Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3 sets x 10 reps
3. Dumbbell Chest Fly – 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Lateral Raise – 3 sets x 15 reps
5. Overhead Tricep Extension – 3 sets x 12 reps

**Day 2 – Lower Body (Quad Focus)**
1. Goblet Squat – 4 sets x 10 reps
2. Bulgarian Split Squat – 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
3. Dumbbell Step-Up – 3 sets x 12 reps per leg
4. Calf Raise (single leg, bodyweight or holding dumbbell) – 3 sets x 15 reps per leg
5. Plank – 3 sets x 45 seconds

**Day 3 – Upper Body (Pull Focus)**
1. Dumbbell Bent-Over Row – 4 sets x 10 reps
2. Single-Arm Dumbbell Row – 3 sets x 10 reps per arm
3. Reverse Fly – 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Dumbbell Bicep Curl – 3 sets x 12 reps
5. Hammer Curl – 3 sets x 12 reps

**Day 4 – Lower Body (Posterior Focus)**
1. Romanian Deadlift – 4 sets x 10 reps
2. Single-Leg RDL – 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
3. Glute Bridge (dumbbell on hips) – 3 sets x 15 reps
4. Lying Leg Curl (if possible with bench setup) or Good Morning – 3 sets x 12 reps
5. Side Plank – 3 sets x 30 seconds per side

**Rest Days:** 3 days per week. Space workouts evenly (e.g., Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri or Mon/Wed/Fri/Sat).

**Progression:** Add 1–2 reps or 0.5–1 kg to dumbbells each week where form stays clean. Track every session.

**Warm-Up (5 min):** Arm circles, bodyweight squats, glute bridges, and light cardio in place.

**Cool-Down (5 min):** Stretch quads, hamstrings, chest, shoulders, and lats. Hold each 20–30 seconds.
