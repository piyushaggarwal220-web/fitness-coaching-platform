**Weekly Structure: 3 Days, 60 Minutes Per Session**

You will train Monday, Wednesday, Friday (or your preferred 3 non-consecutive days). Each session includes a warmup, main work, and cooldown. Aim for 6000-7000 steps daily on training days, 5000+ on rest days. Prioritize sleep hygiene: aim for 6+ hours whenever possible, keep your room dark and cool, and avoid caffeine 6 hours before bed.

**Day 1: Upper Push + Upper Pull**

Warmup (5 min):
- Arm circles 10 each direction
- Cat-cow stretch 10 reps
- Band pull-aparts 15 reps
- Incline treadmill walk 3 min

Main Work (50 min):
1. Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets x 8 reps (rest 90 sec)
2. Seated Cable Row: 4 sets x 8 reps (rest 90 sec)
3. Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets x 10 reps (rest 60 sec)
4. Lat Pulldown (wide grip): 3 sets x 10 reps (rest 60 sec)
5. Dumbbell Lateral Raise: 3 sets x 12 reps (rest 45 sec)
6. Cable Face Pull: 3 sets x 15 reps (rest 45 sec)

Cooldown (5 min):
- Chest doorway stretch 30 sec each side
- Overhead tricep stretch 30 sec each arm
- Child's pose 60 sec
- Slow walk 2 min

**Day 2: Lower Body + Core**

Warmup (5 min):
- Leg swings front-back 10 each leg
- Leg swings side-side 10 each leg
- Bodyweight squat 15 reps
- Glute bridge 15 reps
- Incline treadmill walk 3 min

Main Work (50 min):
1. Barbell Back Squat: 4 sets x 8 reps (rest 2 min)
2. Romanian Deadlift (barbell): 3 sets x 10 reps (rest 90 sec)
3. Leg Press: 3 sets x 12 reps (rest 90 sec)
4. Lying Leg Curl: 3 sets x 12 reps (rest 60 sec)
5. Plank: 3 sets x 30-45 sec (rest 45 sec)
6. Cable Crunch: 3 sets x 15 reps (rest 45 sec)

Cooldown (5 min):
- Standing quad stretch 30 sec each leg
- Seated hamstring stretch 30 sec each leg
- Lying spinal twist 30 sec each side
- Slow walk 2 min

**Day 3: Full Body Circuit + Accessory**

Warmup (5 min):
- Jumping jacks 20 reps
- Inchworm walkout 8 reps
- Bodyweight squat 12 reps
- Push-up (knee or regular) 8 reps
- Incline treadmill walk 2 min

Main Work (50 min):
1. Dumbbell Goblet Squat: 3 sets x 12 reps (rest 60 sec)
2. Push-Up (knee or regular): 3 sets x AMRAP (as many reps as possible, rest 60 sec)
3. Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift: 3 sets x 12 reps (rest 60 sec)
4. Dumbbell Bent-Over Row: 3 sets x 10 reps (rest 60 sec)
5. Dumbbell Step-Up (each leg): 3 sets x 10 reps per leg (rest 60 sec)
6. Cable Woodchop: 3 sets x 12 reps each side (rest 45 sec)

Cooldown (5 min):
- Hip flexor stretch 30 sec each side
- Chest stretch 30 sec each side
- Seated forward fold 60 sec
- Slow walk 2 min

**Rest Days: Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday, Sunday**

Focus on active recovery: light walk 15-20 min, stretching, foam rolling. Aim for 5000+ steps. Prioritize sleep and hydration (2-3L water daily).

**Cardio**

Perform 3 sessions of 30 minutes brisk walking or incline treadmill on non-training days (e.g. Tuesday, Thursday, Sunday). Keep heart rate conversational. This supports fat loss without adding fatigue.

**Progression Notes**

If you complete all sets and reps with good form, add 2-5% load next week on compound lifts (bench, squat, row, RDL). If energy remains low, repeat the same weights and focus on form and consistency. Track your workouts in a notebook or app. Let me know if any exercise causes discomfort.

**Recovery Priority**

Given your current fatigue and sleep debt, do not push to failure. Leave 1-2 reps in reserve on each set. If you feel overly fatigued on any day, reduce volume by 1 set per exercise or lower the weight slightly. Consistency over intensity this week. Address hunger with the updated nutrition plan and consider a small protein-rich snack (roasted chana, paneer cubes, or curd) 1-2 hours pre-workout to stabilize energy.

Stay strong, Divya. Small steps forward each week will get you to your goal.
