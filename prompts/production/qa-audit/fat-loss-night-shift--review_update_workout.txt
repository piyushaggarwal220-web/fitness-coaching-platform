**3-Day Upper/Lower Split – Phase 2**

You've completed your foundation phase. We're now moving to a structured upper/lower split with slightly increased volume (15% bump) while keeping session length around 60 minutes. Given your low energy and sleep debt, we're prioritizing compound movements and managing fatigue carefully.

**Training Schedule:**
- Day 1: Upper Body
- Day 2: Lower Body
- Day 3: Upper Body

**Warm-Up (all days):** 5 min light cardio + dynamic stretches (arm circles, leg swings, cat-cow)

---

**Day 1 – Upper Body (Push Focus)**

1. Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets x 8 reps (2 min rest)
2. Seated Cable Row – 4 sets x 10 reps (90 sec rest)
3. Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3 sets x 10 reps (90 sec rest)
4. Cable Lateral Raise – 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)
5. Dumbbell Bicep Curl – 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)
6. Overhead Tricep Extension (dumbbell) – 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)

**Cool-Down:** 3–5 min walk + upper body static stretches

---

**Day 2 – Lower Body**

1. Barbell Back Squat – 4 sets x 8 reps (2 min rest)
2. Romanian Deadlift (barbell) – 4 sets x 10 reps (2 min rest)
3. Leg Press – 3 sets x 12 reps (90 sec rest)
4. Lying Leg Curl – 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)
5. Standing Calf Raise (dumbbell or machine) – 3 sets x 15 reps (60 sec rest)
6. Plank Hold – 3 sets x 30–45 sec (60 sec rest)

**Cool-Down:** 3–5 min walk + lower body static stretches

---

**Day 3 – Upper Body (Pull Focus)**

1. Barbell Bent-Over Row – 4 sets x 8 reps (2 min rest)
2. Dumbbell Incline Press – 3 sets x 10 reps (90 sec rest)
3. Lat Pulldown (cable) – 3 sets x 10 reps (90 sec rest)
4. Dumbbell Lateral Raise – 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)
5. Cable Face Pull – 3 sets x 15 reps (60 sec rest)
6. Dumbbell Hammer Curl – 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)

**Cool-Down:** 3–5 min walk + upper body static stretches

---

**Progression Notes:**
- Aim to add 1–2 reps or 2.5 kg when you can complete all prescribed reps with good form.
- If energy is very low on a given day, reduce one working set per exercise rather than skipping the session.
- Track your lifts in a notebook or app.

**Recovery Priorities:**
- Target 6.5–7 hours sleep on off-days if possible.
- Keep steps around 5,000–6,000 daily.
- Stay hydrated (2L+ water).

**Cardio:**
- Continue 3 x 30 min brisk walks on non-training days or post-workout if time allows.

You're doing great showing up despite tough shifts. Let's build momentum with this new split.
