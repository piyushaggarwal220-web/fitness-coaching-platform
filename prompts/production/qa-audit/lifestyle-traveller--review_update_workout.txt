**Upper/Lower 4-Day Split – Progression Phase**

You've completed a solid foundation month. We're now moving to an upper/lower split with increased volume (~15% bump) while keeping sessions around 60 minutes. Focus on controlled tempo and progressive overload.

**Day 1 – Upper Push Focus**
• Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets x 6–8 reps
• Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 8–10 reps
• Cable Chest Fly: 3 sets x 12 reps
• Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 4 sets x 8 reps
• Lateral Raise (cables or dumbbells): 3 sets x 12–15 reps
• Overhead Tricep Extension (cable or dumbbell): 3 sets x 10–12 reps

**Day 2 – Lower Quad/Glute Focus**
• Barbell Back Squat: 4 sets x 6–8 reps
• Bulgarian Split Squat (dumbbells): 3 sets x 8–10 reps per leg
• Leg Press (if available) or Goblet Squat: 3 sets x 10–12 reps
• Leg Extension (machine or banded): 3 sets x 12–15 reps
• Standing Calf Raise: 4 sets x 12–15 reps

**Day 3 – Upper Pull Focus**
• Barbell or Dumbbell Row: 4 sets x 6–8 reps
• Lat Pulldown or Assisted Pull-Up: 4 sets x 8–10 reps
• Seated Cable Row: 3 sets x 10–12 reps
• Face Pull (cable): 3 sets x 15 reps
• Barbell or Dumbbell Curl: 3 sets x 10–12 reps
• Hammer Curl: 3 sets x 10–12 reps

**Day 4 – Lower Hamstring/Posterior Focus**
• Romanian Deadlift (barbell or dumbbell): 4 sets x 8 reps
• Lying or Seated Leg Curl: 4 sets x 10–12 reps
• Walking Lunges (dumbbells): 3 sets x 10 steps per leg
• Glute Bridge or Hip Thrust: 3 sets x 12–15 reps
• Seated Calf Raise or Single-Leg Calf Raise: 3 sets x 12–15 reps

**Weekly Structure:**
Mon: Upper Push | Tue: Lower Quad | Thu: Upper Pull | Fri: Lower Hamstring

**Tempo & Rest:**
• Compound lifts (bench, squat, row, RDL): 2–3 min rest
• Isolation moves: 60–90 sec rest
• Control the eccentric (2–3 sec down), explosive concentric

**Progression:**
Aim to add 1–2 reps or 2.5–5 kg every 1–2 weeks on main lifts. Track your top sets.

**Travel Adaptation:**
If hotel gym lacks barbells, substitute with heavy dumbbell variations or increase reps slightly (e.g., 10–12 range). Prioritize the big movements (squat, row, press) and compress accessories if time-limited.
