**Pooja Menon – Progressive Upper/Lower Split**

You've completed your foundation phase with excellent adherence! We're now increasing volume by 15% and shifting to a 2-body-part focus per session to continue your recomposition progress.

**Training Schedule: 3 days per week, ~60 minutes per session**

---

**Day 1 – Upper Body (Push + Pull Focus)**

*Warm-up: 5 min arm circles, band pull-aparts, light cardio*

1. **Dumbbell Bench Press** – 4 sets x 10 reps (2-0-2 tempo)
2. **Dumbbell Bent-Over Rows** – 4 sets x 10 reps (2-0-2 tempo)
3. **Dumbbell Shoulder Press** – 3 sets x 12 reps
4. **Band-Assisted Pull-Aparts** – 3 sets x 15 reps
5. **Dumbbell Bicep Curls** – 3 sets x 12 reps
6. **Overhead Tricep Extension (Dumbbell)** – 3 sets x 12 reps
7. **Plank Hold** – 3 sets x 30–45 seconds

*Cool-down: 3 min stretching – chest, shoulders, lats*

---

**Day 2 – Lower Body (Quad + Glute Focus)**

*Warm-up: 5 min leg swings, bodyweight squats, glute bridges*

1. **Goblet Squats (Dumbbell)** – 4 sets x 10 reps (3-0-1 tempo)
2. **Romanian Deadlifts (Dumbbell)** – 4 sets x 10 reps (3-0-1 tempo)
3. **Walking Lunges (Bodyweight or Dumbbell)** – 3 sets x 12 reps per leg
4. **Single-Leg Glute Bridges** – 3 sets x 12 reps per leg
5. **Band Lateral Walks** – 3 sets x 15 steps each direction
6. **Calf Raises (Dumbbell)** – 3 sets x 15 reps
7. **Dead Bug** – 3 sets x 12 reps per side

*Cool-down: 3 min stretching – quads, hamstrings, hip flexors*

---

**Day 3 – Full Body (Strength + Conditioning)**

*Warm-up: 5 min dynamic stretches, inchworms, high knees*

1. **Push-Ups (Elevated if needed)** – 4 sets x 8–12 reps (AMRAP last set)
2. **Dumbbell Goblet Squats** – 3 sets x 15 reps
3. **Dumbbell Single-Arm Rows** – 3 sets x 12 reps per arm
4. **Reverse Lunges (Dumbbell)** – 3 sets x 12 reps per leg
5. **Band Chest Press** – 3 sets x 15 reps
6. **Plank to Downward Dog** – 3 sets x 10 reps
7. **Bicycle Crunches** – 3 sets x 20 reps

*Cool-down: 3 min full-body stretching*

---

**Progression Notes:**
- Rest 60–90 seconds between sets for compound movements (squats, rows, presses)
- Rest 45–60 seconds for isolation exercises
- Increase dumbbell weight by 1–2 kg when you can complete all sets with good form
- Track your reps and weights in your workout log

**Cardio remains the same:** 3 x 30-minute brisk walks on non-training days or post-workout.
