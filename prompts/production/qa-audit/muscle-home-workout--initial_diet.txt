Weekly Diet Plan: Hey Meera!

Great to have you on board. Since you're aiming for muscle gain over the next 12 weeks, we're going to keep your meals consistent with what you already love—dal, paneer, idli, curd rice—but dial in the portions so you're eating just enough extra to build strength without gaining unnecessary fat. **MONDAY**

**Breakfast (8:30 am)**
2 cups poha (approx 150g) made with 1 tsp oil, mixed with peas, onions, and a squeeze of lemon. Serve with a small bowl of curd (100g) on the side.
(P: 6g | C: 52g | F: 5g | ~280 kcal)

**Lunch (1:30 pm)**
1.5 katori cooked rice (approx 200g), 1 katori cooked dal (approx 150g, masoor or toor), and a small bowl of mixed sabzi (approx 100g—spinach, beans, carrot). Finish with a small pinch of ghee (1 tsp) mixed in.
(P: 16g | C: 68g | F: 8g | ~430 kcal)

**Evening Snack (6:30 pm)**
1 cup chai (made with 1/2 cup milk) with a small handful of roasted chana (approx 30g) and 2 roasted almonds.
(P: 8g | C: 18g | F: 6g | ~150 kcal)

**Dinner (9:00 pm)**
2 rotis (approx 60g), 100g paneer (fresh or homemade), cooked as paneer bhurji with onions and tomatoes, plus a side salad with cucumber, tomato, and a pinch of salt.
(P: 22g | C: 32g | F: 14g | ~360 kcal)

**Quick Tips:**

- **Hydration:** You mentioned 2L water daily—that's a solid baseline. On training days (your 4 evenings), try to sip an extra 500ml around your workout.
- **Oil & Ghee:** I've kept these minimal but present. Don't skip them entirely; a tsp of ghee in dal or rice helps with nutrient absorption and keeps meals satisfying.
- **Chutney & Spices:** Coconut chutney, green chutney, and pickle are your friends—they add flavour with almost zero calories and aid digestion. Make a batch of green chutney on Sunday.
- **Paneer Variety:** You love paneer, so I've rotated it—bhurji, tikka, shahi, do pyaza, fry, matar. Keep it interesting so you don't get bored.
- **Dal Rotation:** Switching between masoor, toor, chana, moong, urad, and arhar keeps your palate fresh and ensures you get different micronutrients.
- **Portion Check:** If you're not seeing progress in 3 weeks, you might be eating slightly more than intended. Weigh your rice and dal once a week to stay honest.

You've got this, Meera. Stick to the timings, keep the portions steady, and pair this with your 4 evening training sessions. Let's build some strength over the next 12 weeks. 💪
