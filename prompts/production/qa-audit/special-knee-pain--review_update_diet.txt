**DAILY NUTRITION STRUCTURE**

**Target: ~1,850 kcal | Protein: ~120g | Carbs: ~180g | Fat: ~55g**
*(Slight deficit maintained, but we're prioritizing volume and protein to crush hunger)*

---

**MEAL 1 – 8:30 AM (Breakfast)**
- 1 cup oats (cooked in water) with 1 tsp jaggery
- 200ml low-fat milk or unsweetened soy milk
- 1 scoop whey protein (mixed in milk or separately with water)
- 1 small banana or 5-6 almonds

*Why: High protein + fiber to stabilize blood sugar and reduce mid-morning cravings.*

---

**MEAL 2 – 11:00 AM (Mid-Morning)**
- 1 small bowl (100g) curd (low-fat) with cucumber and a pinch of salt
- 1 small apple or guava

*Why: Protein + probiotics for gut health; fruit for micronutrients and satiety.*

---

**MEAL 3 – 1:30 PM (Lunch)**
- 1 cup (150g cooked) brown rice or 2 medium chapatis
- 1 cup dal (moong/toor – no tadka, minimal oil)
- 1 cup mixed vegetable sabzi (bottle gourd, spinach, beans—steamed or light sauté, <1 tsp oil)
- 1 small bowl salad (cucumber, tomato, carrot, lemon, no dressing)
- 100g paneer (grilled or steamed, not fried)

*Why: Balanced meal; paneer adds protein, veggies add volume to keep you full.*

---

**MEAL 4 – 4:30 PM (Pre-Workout Snack)**
- 1 small bowl (30g) roasted chana
- 1 cup black coffee or green tea (no sugar)

*Why: Light protein + caffeine for energy before evening training.*

---

**MEAL 5 – 6:30 PM (Post-Workout)**
- 2 idlis (steamed) with 2 tbsp coconut chutney (minimal oil)
- OR 1 medium sweet potato (boiled/roasted)

*Why: Quick-digesting carbs to replenish glycogen post-training.*

---

**MEAL 6 – 9:00 PM (Dinner)**
- 2 medium chapatis (whole wheat, no ghee)
- 1 cup rajma or chana curry (light gravy, <1 tsp oil)
- 1 large bowl mixed salad (lettuce, cucumber, bell pepper, lemon)
- 100g paneer bhurji (dry, minimal oil) OR 1 cup curd

*Why: High protein + fiber to keep you full overnight and support recovery.*

---

**DAILY HYDRATION & EXTRAS**
- **Water:** Minimum 3 liters daily (up from 2L—dehydration amplifies hunger)
- **Herbal tea:** 1 cup chamomile or peppermint before bed (sleep aid)
