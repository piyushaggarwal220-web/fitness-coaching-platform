**Upper/Lower Split – 4 Days per Week**

You've built a solid foundation over the past month. We're now moving to a focused upper/lower split with slightly higher volume (15% increase) to continue fat loss and strength gains while keeping sessions around 60 minutes.

**Training Schedule:**
- Day 1: Upper Body A
- Day 2: Lower Body A
- Day 3: Rest or light activity
- Day 4: Upper Body B
- Day 5: Lower Body B
- Day 6–7: Rest

---

**Day 1 – Upper Body A**
1. Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets x 8 reps (2 min rest)
2. Bent-Over Barbell Row: 4 sets x 8 reps (2 min rest)
3. Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets x 10 reps (90 sec rest)
4. Cable Lat Pulldown: 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)
5. Dumbbell Bicep Curl: 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)
6. Tricep Rope Pushdown: 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)

**Day 2 – Lower Body A**
1. Barbell Back Squat: 4 sets x 8 reps (2 min rest)
2. Romanian Deadlift (Barbell): 4 sets x 10 reps (90 sec rest)
3. Leg Press: 3 sets x 12 reps (90 sec rest)
4. Lying Leg Curl: 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)
5. Standing Calf Raise: 3 sets x 15 reps (60 sec rest)

**Day 3 – Rest or Active Recovery**
Light walk, stretching, or complete rest.

**Day 4 – Upper Body B**
1. Dumbbell Incline Bench Press: 4 sets x 10 reps (90 sec rest)
2. Cable Seated Row: 4 sets x 10 reps (90 sec rest)
3. Dumbbell Lateral Raise: 3 sets x 15 reps (60 sec rest)
4. Cable Face Pull: 3 sets x 15 reps (60 sec rest)
5. Hammer Curl: 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)
6. Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extension: 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)

**Day 5 – Lower Body B**
1. Goblet Squat (Dumbbell): 4 sets x 12 reps (90 sec rest)
2. Barbell Sumo Deadlift: 4 sets x 8 reps (2 min rest)
3. Bulgarian Split Squat (Dumbbells): 3 sets x 10 reps per leg (90 sec rest)
4. Seated Leg Curl: 3 sets x 12 reps (60 sec rest)
5. Seated Calf Raise: 3 sets x 15 reps (60 sec rest)

---

**Progression:**
- Aim to add 1–2 reps or 2.5 kg when you complete all sets with good form.
- Focus on controlled tempo: 2 seconds down, 1 second up.
- Rest days are crucial for recovery and fat loss.

**Notes:**
- All exercises use equipment you have access to (barbell, dumbbells, cables, leg press).
- Squats and rows remain central as requested.
- No burpees or high-impact movements.
- Keep rest periods tight to maintain calorie burn and session length around 60 minutes.

Stay consistent, track your lifts, and let's build momentum together.
