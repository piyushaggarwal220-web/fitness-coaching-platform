**Home Resistance Band Program – Upper/Lower Split**

You're progressing to a 2-body-part split to build on your foundation while keeping sessions manageable given your recent schedule constraints. Each workout targets 2 major muscle groups with slightly increased volume (15% bump). Sessions remain ~45–50 minutes to reduce friction.

**Weekly Structure: 3 days per week, evening sessions**

---

**Day 1: Chest + Back**

1. **Band Chest Press** (anchor at mid-chest height)  
   4 sets × 10 reps  
   *Rest 90 sec between sets*

2. **Band Rows** (anchor at chest height, your favorite)  
   4 sets × 10 reps  
   *Rest 90 sec*

3. **Band Chest Fly** (anchor behind you, arms wide)  
   3 sets × 12 reps  
   *Rest 60 sec*

4. **Band Face Pulls** (anchor high, pull to face)  
   3 sets × 15 reps  
   *Rest 60 sec*

5. **Band Pulldowns** (anchor overhead, pull to chest)  
   3 sets × 12 reps  
   *Rest 60 sec*

---

**Day 2: Legs + Shoulders**

1. **Band Squats** (stand on band, handles at shoulders)  
   4 sets × 10 reps  
   *Rest 90 sec*

2. **Band Overhead Press** (stand on band, press overhead)  
   4 sets × 10 reps  
   *Rest 90 sec*

3. **Band Romanian Deadlift** (stand on band, hinge at hips)  
   3 sets × 12 reps  
   *Rest 75 sec*

4. **Band Lateral Raises**  
   3 sets × 15 reps  
   *Rest 60 sec*

5. **Band Leg Curls** (anchor low, curl heel to glute lying prone)  
   3 sets × 12 reps per leg  
   *Rest 60 sec*

---

**Day 3: Arms + Core**

1. **Band Bicep Curls** (stand on band, curl handles to shoulders)  
   4 sets × 12 reps  
   *Rest 60 sec*

2. **Band Tricep Extensions** (anchor overhead, extend down)  
   4 sets × 12 reps  
   *Rest 60 sec*

3. **Band Hammer Curls** (neutral grip curls)  
   3 sets × 15 reps  
   *Rest 60 sec*

4. **Band Overhead Tricep Extension** (stand on band, extend overhead)  
   3 sets × 15 reps  
   *Rest 60 sec*

5. **Plank Hold**  
   3 sets × 30–45 sec  
   *Rest 45 sec*

6. **Band Pallof Press** (anchor at chest height, resist rotation)  
   3 sets × 10 reps per side  
   *Rest 45 sec*

---

**Progression Notes:**
- Increase band tension (step further on band or use thicker band) when you hit top reps comfortably for 2 consecutive sessions.
- Focus on controlled tempo: 2 sec down, 1 sec up.
- If a session feels too long, drop the last exercise and keep the first 4–5.

**Cardio:** Continue 3× 30-min brisk walks on non-training days or post-workout.

**Key Reminders:**
- Warm up 5 min: arm circles, leg swings, band pull-aparts.
- Prioritize showing up over perfection—even 2 workouts this week beats zero.
- Log your reps/sets to track progress and stay motivated.
