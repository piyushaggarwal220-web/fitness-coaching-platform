**MONDAY**

**Breakfast (8:30 am):** Have 2 cups poha (approx 150g cooked) mixed with 1 small onion, 1 tbsp peanuts (crushed), and a squeeze of lemon. Serve with 1 small glass (200ml) low-fat milk on the side.
(P: 12g | C: 55g | F: 8g | ~320 kcal)

**Mid-morning (10:30 am):** 1 medium apple (150g) with a small handful of roasted almonds (20g, about 10 nuts).
(P: 6g | C: 22g | F: 9g | ~170 kcal)

**Lunch (1:30 pm):** 1.5 cups cooked basmati rice (approx 200g), 1 katori (150g) cooked dal (mix of moong and masoor), and a small bowl of mixed sabzi (carrots, peas, beans — about 100g). Drizzle 1 tsp ghee over the rice.
(P: 18g | C: 72g | F: 8g | ~440 kcal)

**Evening snack (6:30 pm):** 1 cup strong tea (with 50ml low-fat milk, no sugar) paired with a small bowl of roasted chana (50g, about 3 tbsp) and 2 whole wheat biscuits.
(P: 12g | C: 35g | F: 6g | ~250 kcal)

**Dinner (9:00 pm):** 2 medium rotis (approx 60g), 100g paneer bhurji (paneer scrambled with onions, tomatoes, and green chilli), and a side salad of cucumber, tomato, and lettuce with a pinch of salt and lemon.
(P: 22g | C: 32g | F: 18g | ~380 kcal)
