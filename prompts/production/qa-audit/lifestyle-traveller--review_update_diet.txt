**DAILY NUTRITION TARGETS**
- Calories: ~1,850 kcal

- Carbs: 180–200 g

---

**MEAL 1 – BREAKFAST (8:30 AM)**

*Option A:*
- Oats: 50 g (dry weight)
- Milk (low-fat): 200 ml
- Paneer (cubed): 50 g
- Almonds: 8–10 pieces
- Banana or apple: 1 small

*Option B:*
- Idli: 3 medium (steamed)
- Sambar: 1 bowl (lentil-based, light oil)
- Coconut chutney: 2 tbsp
- Boiled egg whites: 3 (or 30 g paneer if you prefer)
- Orange or papaya: 1 small serving

*Option C:*
- Poha: 60 g (dry weight), cooked with minimal oil, peas, peanuts (10 g)
- Curd (low-fat): 150 g
- Flaxseeds: 1 tsp (ground)

---

**MEAL 2 – MID-MORNING SNACK (11:00 AM)**

- Roasted chana: 30 g OR
- Sprouts salad (moong/chana): 1 bowl with lemon, salt, coriander OR
- Whey protein shake: 1 scoop (30 g) with water (if protein target is lagging)

---

**MEAL 3 – LUNCH (1:30 PM)**

*Standard Plate:*
- Rice (cooked): 1 cup (150 g)
- Dal (any variety): 1 bowl (150 ml, minimal ghee)
- Sabzi (non-starchy veg—palak, bhindi, lauki, beans): 1 bowl
- Curd (low-fat): 100 g
- Salad (cucumber, tomato, onion, carrot): Unlimited

*Alternate (2–3 times/week):*
- Swap rice for 2 medium chapatis (whole wheat)
- Add rajma or chole (1 bowl) for protein
- Keep dal and sabzi as above

---

**MEAL 4 – EVENING SNACK (6:30 PM)**

*Option A:*
- Tea (no sugar or jaggery): 1 cup
- Roasted chana: 25 g
- Boiled egg whites: 2 (or 40 g paneer cubes)

*Option B:*
- Buttermilk (low-fat): 1 glass
- Handful of roasted makhana: 20 g
- Carrot/cucumber sticks: Unlimited

**CRITICAL NOTE:** No biscuits, namkeen, or fried snacks. This is where weekends derail you. Pack your snacks when travelling.

---

**MEAL 5 – DINNER (9:00 PM – AIM FOR 8:30 PM IF POSSIBLE)**

*Standard Plate:*
- Roti (whole wheat): 2 medium
- Paneer bhurji or sabzi: 1 bowl (80 g paneer, cooked with minimal oil)
- Dal (optional, if lunch was light): ½ bowl
- Salad (raw veggies): Large portion
- Raita (curd + cucumber): 100 g

*Alternate (3–4 times/week):*
- Curd rice: 1 bowl (150 g rice + 150 g curd)
- Sabzi: 1 bowl (bottle gourd, ridge gourd, spinach)
- Salad: Large portion

**RULE:** If dinner is delayed past 9:30 PM, skip one roti and increase salad volume.

---

**WEEKEND STRATEGY (SATURDAY & SUNDAY)**

This is where you're losing the battle. Weekends require the same discipline as weekdays—no exceptions.

- **Meal timing:** Stick to the same schedule. Don't skip breakfast and overeat at lunch.
- **Eating out:** If unavoidable, choose tandoori paneer, dal tadka, sabzi, and limit rice/roti to half your usual portion. Avoid creamy gravies, fried starters, and desserts.
- **Alcohol:** If you drink, account for it—each peg is ~100 kcal. Limit to 2 drinks max and eat a protein-rich meal beforehand.
- **Social pressure:** Politely decline extras. You're 42, not 22—your health goals matter more than pleasing others.

---

**TRAVEL DAYS (2 WEEKS/MONTH)**

- **Carry:** Roasted chana, makhana, protein powder sachets, instant oats.
- **Hotel breakfast:** Prioritize eggs (if available), idli/dosa with sambar, fruits. Avoid parathas and heavy buffet items.
- **Lunch/Dinner:** Grilled paneer, dal, sabzi, salad. Request less oil. Avoid biryani, fried rice, naan.
- **Hydration:** Carry a 1L bottle. Refill twice daily.
