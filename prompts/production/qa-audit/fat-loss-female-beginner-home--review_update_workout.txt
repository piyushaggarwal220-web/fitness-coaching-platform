**Sneha Iyer – Progressive Upper/Lower Split**

You've crushed the foundation phase with perfect adherence! Time to level up with a two-body-part split while keeping your 3-day, 45-minute structure. Volume increases ~15% to keep driving progress.

**Training Structure:**
- **Day 1 – Upper Body (Push Focus)**
- **Day 2 – Lower Body (Quad Focus)**
- **Day 3 – Upper Body (Pull Focus) + Lower Accessory**

---

**DAY 1: UPPER PUSH**

1. **Resistance Band Chest Press** – 4 sets x 10 reps
   - Anchor band behind you, press forward at chest height
   - 2-0-2 tempo, 60s rest

2. **Pike Push-Ups** – 4 sets x 6–8 reps
   - Hips high, hands shoulder-width, press to overhead position
   - 90s rest

3. **Resistance Band Lateral Raise** – 3 sets x 15 reps
   - Stand on band center, raise handles to shoulder height
   - 45s rest

4. **Overhead Band Tricep Extension** – 3 sets x 12 reps
   - Anchor band overhead, extend arms down
   - 45s rest

5. **Plank Hold** – 3 sets x 30–40 seconds
   - Forearms on mat, body straight
   - 45s rest

**Total Time:** ~42 minutes

---

**DAY 2: LOWER QUAD**

1. **Goblet Squat (Band Resistance)** – 4 sets x 10 reps
   - Hold water jug or heavy object at chest, band around thighs for resistance
   - 3-0-1 tempo, 90s rest

2. **Bulgarian Split Squat (Bodyweight)** – 4 sets x 8 reps per leg
   - Rear foot elevated on mat/step, descend until front thigh parallel
   - 90s rest between legs

3. **Resistance Band Leg Extension** – 3 sets x 15 reps
   - Sit on floor, loop band around ankle and anchor point, extend knee
   - 60s rest

4. **Glute Bridge (Band Around Knees)** – 3 sets x 15 reps
   - Squeeze glutes at top, 2-second hold
   - 45s rest

5. **Standing Calf Raise** – 3 sets x 20 reps
   - Bodyweight or hold water jugs
   - 45s rest

**Total Time:** ~44 minutes

---

**DAY 3: UPPER PULL + LOWER ACCESSORY**

1. **Resistance Band Rows** – 4 sets x 10 reps
   - Anchor band at waist height, pull elbows back
   - 2-0-2 tempo, 60s rest

2. **Inverted Rows (Under Table)** – 4 sets x 6–8 reps
   - Lie under sturdy table, pull chest to edge
   - 90s rest

3. **Resistance Band Face Pulls** – 3 sets x 15 reps
   - Band at eye level, pull to face with elbows high
   - 45s rest

4. **Band Bicep Curls** – 3 sets x 12 reps
   - Stand on band, curl handles to shoulders
   - 45s rest

5. **Romanian Deadlift (Band Resistance)** – 3 sets x 12 reps
   - Stand on band, hinge at hips, feel hamstring stretch
   - 60s rest

6. **Dead Bug** – 2 sets x 10 reps per side
   - Alternate lowering opposite arm and leg
   - 30s rest

**Total Time:** ~43 minutes

---

**CARDIO PLAN:**
- 3 sessions per week x 30 minutes brisk walking (continue current schedule)
- Optional: add 5 minutes to one session if energy permits

**PROGRESSION NOTES:**
- When you hit the top of rep ranges for 2 consecutive sessions, increase band resistance or add a 2-second pause at peak contraction
- Focus on controlled tempo – no rushing through reps
- Rest days: Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday/Sunday (adjust to your schedule)

**FORM CUES:**
- Keep core braced on all movements
- Full range of motion – no partial reps
- Breathe: exhale on effort, inhale on return
