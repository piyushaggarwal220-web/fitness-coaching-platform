**Weekly Structure:**
3 training days, 3 rest/active recovery days
Target: 6000–7000 steps daily
Continue 3x 30-min brisk walks on non-training days

**Day 1 – Upper Body (Push + Pull Focus):**
Warmup: 5 min arm circles, band pull-aparts, light squats (2 sets x 10 reps)
1. Resistance Band Rows (favorite exercise) — 4 sets x 10 reps
2. Resistance Band Chest Press (standing or kneeling) — 4 sets x 10 reps
3. Resistance Band Lateral Raises — 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Resistance Band Bicep Curls — 3 sets x 12 reps
5. Resistance Band Tricep Extensions (overhead) — 3 sets x 12 reps
Cooldown: 5 min stretching — chest, shoulders, arms

**Day 2 – Lower Body + Core:**
Warmup: 5 min leg swings, glute bridges, bodyweight squats (2 sets x 10 reps)
1. Bodyweight Squats (favorite exercise) — 4 sets x 12 reps
2. Resistance Band Romanian Deadlifts — 4 sets x 10 reps
3. Resistance Band Glute Bridges — 3 sets x 15 reps
4. Resistance Band Lateral Walks — 3 sets x 12 steps each side
5. Plank Hold — 3 sets x 30–45 seconds
Cooldown: 5 min stretching — quads, hamstrings, glutes

**Day 3 – Full Body (Upper + Lower Integration):**
Warmup: 5 min dynamic stretches, band pull-aparts, leg swings (2 sets x 10 reps)
1. Push-Ups (knees or toes) — 3 sets x AMRAP (as many reps as possible)
2. Resistance Band Squats — 3 sets x 15 reps
3. Resistance Band Rows — 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Resistance Band Deadlifts (stiff-leg variation) — 3 sets x 12 reps
5. Resistance Band Shoulder Press (standing) — 3 sets x 12 reps
6. Bicycle Crunches — 3 sets x 20 reps total
Cooldown: 5 min full-body stretching

**Rest Days (4 days):**
Active recovery: 30-min brisk walk, light yoga on mat, foam rolling if available
Focus on sleep (7–8 hours), hydration (2L+ water), and meal prep

**Progression Notes:**
- Volume increased ~15% (added sets/reps) to match your strong performance.
- Rest 60–90 seconds between sets; focus on controlled tempo (2 sec down, 1 sec up).
- If any exercise feels too easy by the third set, increase band resistance or slow the tempo.
- Track your reps — aim to match or beat them each week.
