**DAILY STRUCTURE: ~2050 kcal | 135g protein | 230g carbs | 65g fat**

**BREAKFAST (8:30 AM) – 450 kcal | 18g protein**
- 1 cup oats (40g) + 200ml whole milk
- 1 whole egg + 2 egg whites scrambled
- 1 tsp ghee for cooking
- Handful berries or banana
*Why: Protein + complex carbs + fibre = stable energy & satiety*

**MID-MORNING SNACK (11:00 AM) – 200 kcal | 15g protein**
- 150g Greek yogurt (0% or 2%)
- 2 tbsp granola
- 1 tbsp honey
*Why: Protein boost before lunch; prevents afternoon hunger*

**LUNCH (1:30 PM) – 550 kcal | 28g protein**
- 1.5 cups cooked basmati rice
- 1 cup cooked dal (mix moong + masoor for variety)
- 100g paneer curry (light oil, tomato-based)
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (spinach, carrot, peas)
- Small side salad with lemon
*Why: Your favourite base + extra protein from paneer; fibre from dal & veg*

**EVENING SNACK (6:30 PM) – 220 kcal | 12g protein**
- 1 cup tea with 50ml low-fat milk
- 40g roasted chana (your current habit—keep it)
- 1 small roti or 2 rice cakes
*Why: Maintains your routine; adds protein to prevent hunger spike*

**DINNER (9:00 PM) – 550 kcal | 28g protein**
- 2 medium roti (whole wheat)
- 120g paneer (grilled or curry)
- 1.5 cups mixed sabzi (broccoli, beans, capsicum, onion)
- 150g boiled chickpeas OR 100g soya chunks (alternate days)
- 1 tbsp low-fat yogurt as side
*Why: High protein + fibre from legumes; keeps you full overnight*

**OPTIONAL LATE SNACK (if hungry after 10 PM) – 100 kcal | 10g protein**
- 1 cup warm milk with turmeric
- OR 50g paneer
