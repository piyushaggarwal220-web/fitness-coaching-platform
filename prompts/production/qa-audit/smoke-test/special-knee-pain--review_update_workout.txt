**Weekly Structure:**

---

**Day 1: Upper Body Focus**

*Warmup (8 minutes):*
- 3 minutes brisk walk or cycle
- Arm circles: 10 forward, 10 backward
- Band pull-aparts: 2 sets x 15 reps
- Incline push-ups (against bench): 2 sets x 8 reps

*Main Workout:*
1. Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets x 10 reps
   - Rest 90 seconds between sets
   - Use a weight you can control smoothly; focus on full range of motion

2. Seated Cable Row (your favorite!): 3 sets x 10 reps
   - Rest 90 seconds
   - Squeeze shoulder blades together at the end of each rep

3. Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets x 10 reps
   - Rest 90 seconds
   - Keep core tight, avoid arching lower back

4. Dumbbell Lateral Raise: 3 sets x 12 reps
   - Rest 60 seconds
   - Light weight, controlled tempo

5. Cable Tricep Pushdown: 2 sets x 12 reps
   - Rest 60 seconds

6. Dumbbell Bicep Curl: 2 sets x 12 reps
   - Rest 60 seconds

*Cooldown (5 minutes):*
- Chest doorway stretch: 30 seconds each side
- Overhead tricep stretch: 30 seconds each arm
- Seated forward fold: 1 minute
- Deep breathing: 2 minutes

---

**Day 2: Lower Body Focus (Knee-Friendly)**

*Warmup (8 minutes):*
- 3 minutes stationary bike (light resistance)
- Bodyweight glute bridges: 2 sets x 12 reps
- Leg swings: 10 forward/back each leg, 10 side-to-side each leg
- Bodyweight quarter squats: 2 sets x 10 reps

*Main Workout:*
1. Leg Press (knee-safe depth): 3 sets x 12 reps
   - Rest 2 minutes
   - Stop at 90 degrees or where knees feel comfortable — no deep flexion
   - Feet hip-width, toes slightly out

2. Romanian Deadlift (Dumbbell or Barbell): 3 sets x 10 reps
   - Rest 90 seconds
   - Hinge at hips, slight knee bend, feel hamstring stretch

3. Lying Leg Curl: 3 sets x 12 reps
   - Rest 90 seconds
   - Controlled tempo, squeeze at the top

4. Seated or Standing Calf Raise: 3 sets x 15 reps
   - Rest 60 seconds

5. Plank Hold: 2 sets x 30 seconds
   - Rest 60 seconds
   - Keep hips level, breathe steadily

6. Glute Bridge (bodyweight or add dumbbell on hips): 2 sets x 15 reps
   - Rest 60 seconds

*Cooldown (5 minutes):*
- Lying hamstring stretch: 30 seconds each leg
- Figure-4 glute stretch: 30 seconds each side
- Child's pose: 1 minute
- Deep breathing: 2 minutes

---

**Day 3: Full Body Circuit (Lower Volume)**

*Warmup (8 minutes):*
- 3 minutes brisk walk or bike
- Bodyweight squats (quarter depth): 2 sets x 10 reps
- Arm circles: 10 forward, 10 backward
- Cat-cow stretch: 10 reps

*Main Workout (Circuit Style — move through exercises with minimal rest, then rest 2 minutes between rounds):*

Complete 3 rounds:

1. Goblet Squat (dumbbell, partial depth): 10 reps
   - Stop at comfortable depth for knees

2. Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift: 10 reps

3. Dumbbell Row (single-arm, supported on bench): 10 reps each arm

4. Dumbbell Floor Press or Push-Ups (incline if needed): 8–10 reps

5. Dumbbell Reverse Lunge (MODIFIED: Step back only a few inches, keep most weight on front leg to protect knee): 8 reps each leg
   - If any knee discomfort, replace with Glute Bridge: 12 reps

6. Plank Hold: 20 seconds

*Rest 2 minutes after completing all 6 exercises, then repeat for 3 total rounds.*

*Cooldown (5 minutes):*
- Standing quad stretch: 30 seconds each leg
- Seated forward fold: 1 minute
- Chest and shoulder stretch: 1 minute
- Deep breathing: 2 minutes
