Sneha, fantastic work this week! Perfect adherence and strong training performance show you're ready to progress. Since you've been following the single-body-part-per-day split, we're now moving to a 2-body-part split to increase training density while keeping your 45-minute sessions. Volume is increased by approximately 15% to continue your fat loss momentum.

**WEEKLY STRUCTURE:**

Day 1 (Monday/Tuesday): Upper Body Push + Pull
Day 2 (Thursday/Friday): Lower Body + Core
Day 3 (Saturday/Sunday): Full Body Circuit

**WARM-UP (all days):** 5 minutes dynamic stretching — arm circles, leg swings, bodyweight squats, cat-cow stretches

**DAY 1: UPPER BODY PUSH + PULL**

1. Resistance Band Rows (favorite exercise) — 4 sets x 10 reps
   - Anchor band at chest height, pull elbows back, squeeze shoulder blades
   - Rest 60 seconds between sets

2. Resistance Band Chest Press — 4 sets x 10 reps
   - Anchor band behind you, press forward at chest level
   - Rest 60 seconds

3. Resistance Band Lateral Raises — 3 sets x 15 reps
   - Stand on band center, raise arms to shoulder height
   - Rest 45 seconds

4. Resistance Band Bicep Curls — 3 sets x 12 reps
   - Stand on band, curl handles to shoulders
   - Rest 45 seconds

5. Resistance Band Tricep Extensions — 3 sets x 12 reps
   - Anchor band overhead, extend arms downward
   - Rest 45 seconds

**Cooldown:** 3 minutes stretching — chest, shoulders, arms

**DAY 2: LOWER BODY + CORE**

1. Bodyweight Squats (favorite exercise) — 5 sets x 12 reps
   - Bodyweight or hold band for resistance
   - Rest 60 seconds between sets

2. Resistance Band Romanian Deadlifts — 4 sets x 12 reps
   - Stand on band, hinge at hips, feel hamstring stretch
   - Rest 60 seconds

3. Resistance Band Glute Bridges — 4 sets x 15 reps
   - Band across hips, squeeze glutes at top
   - Rest 45 seconds

4. Resistance Band Standing Leg Curls — 3 sets x 12 reps each leg
   - Loop band around ankle, curl heel to glutes
   - Rest 45 seconds

5. Plank Hold — 3 sets x 30-45 seconds
   - On yoga mat, maintain straight line from head to heels
   - Rest 45 seconds

**Cooldown:** 3 minutes stretching — quads, hamstrings, glutes, lower back

**DAY 3: FULL BODY CIRCUIT**

Perform 4 rounds of the following circuit with 90 seconds rest between rounds:

1. Push-Ups — 10-12 reps (knees down if needed)
2. Resistance Band Rows — 12 reps
3. Bodyweight Squats — 15 reps
4. Resistance Band Shoulder Press — 12 reps
5. Mountain Climbers — 20 total reps (10 each leg)
6. Bicycle Crunches — 20 total reps

**Cooldown:** 5 minutes full body stretching on yoga mat

**CARDIO PLAN:**
- 3 sessions x 30 minutes brisk walking (non-training days or post-workout)
- Target: 6000-7000 steps daily (up from 5000)

**REST & RECOVERY:**
- 4 rest days per week
- Prioritize 7-8 hours sleep
- Stay hydrated — 2-2.5L water daily

**PROGRESSION NOTES:**
- If all sets feel easy (RPE 6 or below), increase band resistance or add 2 reps next week
- Focus on controlled tempo: 2 seconds down, 1 second up
- Track your reps in each set to monitor progress

You've earned this progression with your perfect adherence. The 2-body-part split will challenge your muscles in new ways while keeping workouts efficient. Let's keep this momentum going!
