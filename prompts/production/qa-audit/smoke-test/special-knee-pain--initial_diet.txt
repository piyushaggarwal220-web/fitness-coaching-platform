**Day 1 (Monday)**

Breakfast (8:30am): Have 1.5 cups poha (about 60g raw) cooked with peas, peanuts (15g), and a squeeze of lemon, or go for 3 medium idlis (approx 150g total) with 2 tbsp coconut chutney and 1 small bowl sambar (100ml).

Evening Snack (6:30pm): Tea with 30g roasted chana — exactly what you're already doing, so no change here.
(P: 6g | C: 16g | F: 2g | ~105 kcal)

Dinner (9:00pm): 2 medium rotis (approx 60g total), 100g paneer bhurji or paneer tikka (dry, minimal oil), a big bowl of salad (cucumber, tomato, onion, sprouts — about 150g), and 1 small katori raita (100g) on the side.
(P: 28g | C: 38g | F: 16g | ~410 kcal)
