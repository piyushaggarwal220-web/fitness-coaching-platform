**Quick tips as you go:**

- **Oil & ghee:** I've measured 1 tsp per cooked sabzi — that's your cooking fat. Don't add extra; it adds up fast.
- **Dal tip:** Soak dal for 30 mins before cooking to cut cooking time and improve digestion. Add a pinch of asafoetida (hing) to moong and urad dal to reduce bloating.
- **Paneer:** Buy from a trusted vendor and use within 2–3 days. If budget gets tight one week, swap 100g paneer for 120g soya chunks — same protein, cheaper.
- **Roasted chana:** Buy raw chana, roast it at home on a tawa — saves money and tastes fresher. Store in an airtight container.
- **Curd:** Make it at home if you can; it's cheaper and fresher. A 500ml pack will cover your daily needs.
- **Consistency is key:** Stick to the portions and timings. Your body will adjust in 2–3 weeks, and hunger will settle down.

You've got this, Suresh. Eat clean, train hard, and let's hit that 62 kg target. Reach out if anything feels off or you need a swap.
