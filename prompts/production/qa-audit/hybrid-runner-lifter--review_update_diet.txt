Weekly Diet Plan: **ADITYA'S UPDATED NUTRITION PLAN**

Let me be direct: 5 hours of sleep, high cravings, and skipped workouts tell me your body is screaming for fuel and rest. You're training hard but undereating—that's a recipe for burnout, not athletic performance. We're fixing this NOW.

**DAILY CALORIE TARGET: 2,300 kcal | Protein: 145g | Carbs: 290g | Fat: 65g**

---

**MEAL 1 — 8:30 AM (Breakfast)**
- 1 cup oats cooked in 300ml full-fat milk
- 1 scoop whey protein (mixed in or separate)
- 1 banana
- 10 almonds
- 1 tsp ghee drizzled on oats

*Calories: ~550 | Protein: 35g*

---

**MEAL 2 — 11:30 AM (Mid-Morning)**
- 2 whole moong dal chillas (add chopped onion, tomato, coriander)
- 100g paneer cubes (lightly pan-fried or raw)
- 1 small bowl curd

*Calories: ~450 | Protein: 30g*

---

**MEAL 3 — 1:30 PM (Lunch)**
- 1.5 cups cooked white rice OR 4 medium rotis
- 1 bowl dal (moong/toor—200ml)
- 1 bowl mixed veg sabzi (beans, carrots, capsicum—avoid mushrooms)
- 1 small bowl curd
- Side salad: cucumber, tomato, onion, lemon

*Calories: ~650 | Protein: 25g*

---

**MEAL 4 — 4:30 PM (Pre-Workout Snack)**
- 2 idlis with coconut chutney OR 1 masala dosa (small)
- 1 banana
- Black coffee (optional)

*Calories: ~350 | Protein: 10g*

---

**MEAL 5 — 6:30 PM (Post-Workout)**
- 1 scoop whey protein shake with 250ml milk
- 30g roasted chana OR 1 small sweet potato (boiled)

*Calories: ~300 | Protein: 30g*

---

**MEAL 6 — 9:00 PM (Dinner)**
- 3-4 rotis (whole wheat)
- 150g paneer bhurji OR paneer tikka
- 1 bowl mixed veg sabzi (different from lunch)
- Large side salad with 1 tsp olive oil dressing
- 1 small bowl curd

*Calories: ~600 | Protein: 35g*

---

**BEFORE BED (10:30 PM)**
- 1 glass warm milk with a pinch of turmeric OR 100g Greek yogurt

*Calories: ~100 | Protein: 8g*

---

**WEEKLY ADJUSTMENTS:**
- **2 days/week:** Replace 1 meal's paneer with 4 whole eggs (boiled/omelette) for variety.
- **Rest days:** Drop the 4:30 PM snack, move those calories to breakfast (add 1 extra roti or 50g oats).

---

**HYDRATION & ESSENTIALS:**
- **Water:** Minimum 3.5L daily (you're very active—2L is NOT enough).
- **Electrolytes:** Add a pinch of salt + lemon to 1L of water during/after training.
- **Creatine:** 5g daily with post-workout shake (non-negotiable for performance goals).

**WHAT YOU NEED TO HEAR:**

1. **Sleep is non-negotiable.** 5 hours is sabotaging everything—your recovery, hunger hormones, performance, fat loss. Set a 10:30 PM alarm to START winding down. Aim for 7 hours minimum. If work is the issue, we need to talk about priorities.

2. **High hunger = underfueling.** You're burning ~2,800 kcal/day with 12k steps + 5 gym sessions + running. Eating 2,000 kcal is why you're exhausted and craving junk. This plan adds 300 kcal strategically—more carbs around training, more protein to kill hunger.

5. **Cravings = biology, not weakness.** When you sleep 5 hours, ghrelin (hunger hormone) spikes and leptin (fullness hormone) tanks. You're fighting your own hormones. More food + more sleep = cravings disappear.
