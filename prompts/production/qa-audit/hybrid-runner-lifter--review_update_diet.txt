1. **Sleep is your #1 priority.** 5 hours is destroying your recovery, energy, and hunger regulation. Aim for 7.5–8 hours minimum. Set a 10 PM wind-down alarm, no screens 30 min before bed, and use the bedtime snack to stabilize blood sugar overnight.

2. **Pre-workout carbs are non-negotiable.** The banana + dates 30–45 min before training will fuel performance and reduce post-session crashes. Don't skip this — your 5/10 training performance will improve.

3. **Manage weekend adherence.** Days 6–7 are simplified with fewer meals to prep. Batch-cook rajma, chana, and paneer on Saturday morning. If you hit 6/10 again, we need to talk about meal prep strategy or further simplification.

4. **Track your hunger daily.** If it stays above 7/10 mid-week, add 100 kcal via nuts or an extra roti at dinner. High hunger + low energy = you're underfueling for your activity level, and that's a recipe for burnout.

5. **Hydration and electrolytes.** You're averaging 2L water, but with 12,000 steps + gym + very active lifestyle, push for 3–3.5L. Add a pinch of salt to your chaas or buttermilk to retain fluids and support performance.
