**Updated Training Plan – Progressive Split**

You've been running a single-muscle-focus split, so we're now moving to a **2-body-part push/pull/legs structure** to increase training density while keeping sessions around 30 minutes. Volume is increased by ~15% through added sets. All exercises remain bodyweight or minimal equipment to match your home setup.

---

**Day 1 – Push (Chest + Shoulders)**
1. Push-Ups – 4 sets x 10–12 reps (or knees if needed)
2. Pike Push-Ups – 3 sets x 8–10 reps
3. Incline Push-Ups (hands elevated) – 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Plank Shoulder Taps – 3 sets x 20 total taps

**Day 2 – Pull + Core (Back + Abs)**
1. Bodyweight Rows (use table edge or sturdy chair) – 4 sets x 8–10 reps
2. Reverse Snow Angels (lying face down) – 3 sets x 12 reps
3. Superman Holds – 3 sets x 20 seconds
4. Dead Bug – 3 sets x 12 reps per side

**Day 3 – Lower (Quads + Glutes + Hamstrings)**
1. Bodyweight Squats – 4 sets x 15 reps (tempo: 3 sec down, 1 sec up)
2. Bulgarian Split Squats (rear foot elevated on chair) – 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
3. Glute Bridges – 4 sets x 15 reps (2 sec squeeze at top)
4. Wall Sit – 3 sets x 30 seconds

---

**Rest & Progression**
- Rest 60–90 seconds between sets.
- Perform these 3 days per week (e.g., Mon/Wed/Fri).
- Once you hit the top of the rep range for 2 consecutive sessions, slow the tempo or add a pause to increase difficulty.
- Avoid burpees and jumping movements as requested.

---

**Warm-Up (5 min before each session)**
- Arm circles, leg swings, cat-cow stretches, and 20 bodyweight squats.

**Cool-Down (3 min post-workout)**
- Child's pose, quad stretch, hamstring stretch, chest opener.

---

This plan pairs two muscle groups per day to maximize efficiency, keeps you in the 30-minute window, and builds on your foundation phase. Focus on controlled reps and full range of motion.
