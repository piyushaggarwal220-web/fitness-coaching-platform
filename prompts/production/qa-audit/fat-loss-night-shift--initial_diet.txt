Weekly Diet Plan: Hey Divya!

I've put together your first week of eating to support your fat loss goal — we're working with your night shift schedule and keeping things simple with foods you actually enjoy. Since you're often skipping lunch after your shift, I've structured this so you get solid nutrition around your working hours without forcing meals when you're exhausted. Let's aim for consistency this week, even if portions aren't perfect every single day.

**Daily targets we're aiming for:** ~1750 kcal | Protein: ~95g | Carbs: ~215g | Fat: ~48g

**DAY 1**

**Breakfast (7:00 am, post-shift):**
Have 2 medium parathas (whole wheat, around 80g total) with 1 tablespoon ghee, alongside 1 cup (200ml) chai made with full-fat milk and jaggery. Add a small bowl of curd (100g) on the side — it'll help you wind down before sleep.
(P: 12g | C: 58g | F: 14g | ~400 kcal)

**Lunch/Evening Meal (2:00 pm):**
Since you're usually asleep, keep this light if you wake up hungry — 1 bowl of curd rice (150g cooked rice mixed with 100g curd) and a small banana. If you're not hungry, totally fine to skip and move straight to your evening snack.
(P: 8g | C: 52g | F: 4g | ~270 kcal)

**Evening Snack (6:30 pm, pre-shift):**
1 cup chai with 30g roasted chana (about 2 tablespoons). Add 1 small apple or orange if you're still hungry.
(P: 8g | C: 22g | F: 3g | ~145 kcal)

**Dinner (8:00 pm, pre-shift):**
1.5 katori cooked rice (approx 180g) with 1 katori sambar (150g, made with toor dal and veggies like drumstick, carrot, brinjal) and a small portion of beans poriyal or cabbage sabzi (80g). This gives you steady energy through your shift.
(P: 18g | C: 78g | F: 6g | ~430 kcal)

**Night Shift Snack (around 2:00 am):**
1 small idli (40g) with coconut chutney, or a handful of roasted peanuts (20g) with black coffee or green tea.
(P: 6g | C: 18g | F: 8g | ~160 kcal)

**Day 1 Total: P: 52g | C: 228g | F: 35g | ~1405 kcal**
