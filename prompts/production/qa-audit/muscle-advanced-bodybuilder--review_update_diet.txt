KEY RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE):

1. **Calories matter.** You're at 92.3kg and need to drop to 62kg. At 1850 kcal, you'll lose ~0.5kg/week. Track every meal.

2. **Protein stays high (140g).** Protect muscle during the cut. Prioritize chicken, fish, paneer, eggs, dal.

3. **Oil/ghee:** Use only 1 tbsp per meal max. Measure it. No "eyeballing."

4. **Hydration:** Minimum 3L water daily. More on training days.

5. **Meal timing:** Stick to your natural pattern (breakfast 8:30, lunch 1:30, dinner 9:00). Consistency aids adherence.

6. **Dal & paneer:** Your favorites—use them as protein anchors every day. Rotate dal types for variety.

7. **No mushrooms:** Respected. Substitute with extra spinach, broccoli, or cauliflower.

8. **Cheat meal:** Once per week (Saturday or Sunday evening), 500 extra kcal allowed if you've tracked perfectly all week.
