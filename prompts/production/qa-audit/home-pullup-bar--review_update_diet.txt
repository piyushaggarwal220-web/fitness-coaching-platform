**MONDAY**

**Breakfast (8:30 AM)** — 350 kcal
- 1 cup poha (dry measure)
- 1 tbsp ghee
- 1 small onion, chopped
- Pinch turmeric, salt
- 1 cup skimmed milk on the side

**Mid-Morning Snack (11:00 AM)** — 180 kcal
- 1 cup low-fat curd (plain, unsweetened)
- 1 tbsp roasted chana
- 1 tsp honey

**Lunch (1:30 PM)** — 520 kcal
- 1.5 cups cooked basmati rice
- 1 cup dal (moong or masoor, cooked)
- 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas)
- 1 tsp oil for cooking
- Small side salad (cucumber, tomato, lemon)

**Evening Snack (6:30 PM)** — 200 kcal
- 1 cup tea (with 30ml low-fat milk, no sugar)
- 30g roasted chana
- 1 small roti (whole wheat)

**Dinner (9:00 PM)** — 600 kcal
- 2 medium rotis (whole wheat)
- 150g paneer (grilled or lightly pan-fried with 1 tsp oil)
- 1 cup mixed vegetable curry (tomato-based, light spices)
- Side salad (cucumber, tomato, onion, lemon juice)
