**WEEKLY STRUCTURE (4 days, 60 min each)**

**Day 1: Upper Power + Pull**
Warmup: 5 min light cardio + arm circles, band pull-aparts
- Pull-ups: 5 sets x 5 reps (strength focus)
- Barbell rows: 4 sets x 6 reps
- Dips: 4 sets x 8 reps
- Lateral raise: 4 sets x 12 reps (increased from 3 sets)
Cooldown: 5 min stretch

**Day 2: Lower Power**
Warmup: 5 min light cardio + leg swings, bodyweight squats
- Goblet squat: 4 sets x 8 reps (increased from 3x12)
- Romanian deadlift: 4 sets x 8 reps (increased from 3x10)
- Leg curl (resistance band or bodyweight): 4 sets x 12 reps (increased from 3 sets)
Cooldown: 5 min stretch

**Day 3: Upper Hypertrophy + Push**
Warmup: 5 min light cardio + shoulder rolls, push-up hold
- Push-ups: 5 sets x 12 reps (increased volume)
- Incline push-ups: 3 sets x 10 reps (new angle)
- Dumbbell or water jug rows: 3 sets x 12 reps
- Face pulls (band): 3 sets x 15 reps
Cooldown: 5 min stretch

**Day 4: Lower Hypertrophy + Posterior Chain**
Warmup: 5 min light cardio + hip circles, glute bridges
- Goblet squat: 4 sets x 12 reps
- Single-leg RDL (assisted): 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
- Glute bridge holds: 3 sets x 15 reps
- Step-ups (on bench): 3 sets x 12 reps per leg
Cooldown: 5 min stretch

**Cardio:** 3x 30 min brisk walk (maintain current pace)

**Rest Days:** 3 full rest days; prioritize 7–8 hours sleep and hydration.
