**Upper/Lower Split – Progressive Intensity**

You will train 3 days per week, alternating between upper-body and lower-body sessions. This split allows better recovery and targets each muscle group twice over two weeks. All sessions remain ~60 minutes.

---

**Day 1: Upper Body (Push Focus)**

1. **Incline Dumbbell Press** – 4 sets × 8 reps
   - Rest 90 sec between sets
   - Focus on controlled tempo (2-0-2)

2. **Cable Seated Row** – 4 sets × 10 reps
   - Rest 90 sec
   - Pull elbows back, squeeze shoulder blades

3. **Dumbbell Shoulder Press** – 3 sets × 10 reps
   - Rest 60 sec

4. **Cable Lateral Raise** – 3 sets × 12 reps
   - Rest 60 sec
   - Light weight, controlled movement

5. **Overhead Tricep Extension (Cable)** – 3 sets × 12 reps
   - Rest 60 sec

6. **Plank Hold** – 3 sets × 30 sec
   - Rest 45 sec

---

**Day 2: Lower Body (Quad & Glute Focus)**

1. **Goblet Squat** – 4 sets × 10 reps
   - Rest 90 sec
   - Squat to parallel only (knee-friendly depth)

2. **Leg Press** – 4 sets × 12 reps
   - Rest 90 sec
   - Feet shoulder-width, controlled descent

3. **Romanian Deadlift (Dumbbell)** – 3 sets × 10 reps
   - Rest 90 sec
   - Hinge at hips, slight knee bend

4. **Seated Leg Curl** – 3 sets × 12 reps
   - Rest 60 sec

5. **Glute Bridge (Barbell)** – 3 sets × 15 reps
   - Rest 60 sec
   - Squeeze glutes at top

6. **Standing Calf Raise** – 3 sets × 15 reps
   - Rest 45 sec

---

**Day 3: Upper Body (Pull Focus)**

1. **Lat Pulldown (Cable)** – 4 sets × 10 reps
   - Rest 90 sec
   - Pull to upper chest, lean back slightly

2. **Dumbbell Chest Press (Flat Bench)** – 4 sets × 8 reps
   - Rest 90 sec

3. **Face Pulls (Cable)** – 3 sets × 15 reps
   - Rest 60 sec
   - Pull toward face, external rotation

4. **Dumbbell Bicep Curl** – 3 sets × 12 reps
   - Rest 60 sec

5. **Cable Wood Chop** – 3 sets × 12 reps per side
   - Rest 60 sec
   - Rotate through core

6. **Dead Bug** – 3 sets × 10 reps per side
   - Rest 45 sec

---

**Progression Notes:**
- Aim to add 1–2 reps or 2.5 kg when you complete all prescribed reps with good form.
- If energy is low, reduce one set per exercise but maintain intensity.
- Warm up with 5 minutes light cardio + dynamic stretches (leg swings, arm circles).
- Cool down with 5 minutes stretching (quads, hamstrings, chest, lats).

**Injury Modifications:**
- Goblet squat and leg press are knee-friendly; stop if sharp pain occurs.
- Avoid any jumping or deep squat variations.
- If knee discomfort persists, substitute leg press for more glute bridges or step-ups (low step).

**Weekly Structure Example:**
- Week A: Mon (Day 1), Wed (Day 2), Fri (Day 3)
- Week B: Mon (Day 2), Wed (Day 3), Fri (Day 1)

This ensures balanced frequency across muscle groups every two weeks.
