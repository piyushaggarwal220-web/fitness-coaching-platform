Your new split progresses from single body parts to a 2-body-part structure, rotating between Upper/Lower and Full Body sessions. You'll maintain your 60-minute session time while introducing new exercise variations to shock your muscles after 4 weeks on the foundation phase. Volume increases by 15% through additional sets on compound movements. Continue with your favorite squats and rows as anchors, and avoid burpees as requested.

Day 1 – Upper A: Barbell rows 4x8, incline dumbbell bench 4x8, cable lateral raise 3x12, barbell curls 3x10.

Day 2 – Lower A: Barbell squat 4x8, leg press 4x8, leg curl 3x12, calf raises 3x15.

Day 3 – Upper B: Dumbbell bench press 4x8, barbell bent-over rows 4x8, cable chest fly 3x12, tricep rope pushdown 3x10.

Day 4 – Lower B: Goblet squat 3x10, Romanian deadlift 3x10, leg extension 3x12, walking lunges 3x10 per leg.

Cardio: Continue 3x 30-minute brisk walks on non-consecutive days. Maintain even spacing throughout the week.

High hunger Thu-Sun signals calorie deficit. Add protein/fiber to breakfast (Greek yogurt + poha), lunch (extra dal), and snacks (roasted chana + paneer). Distribute weekend meals consistently—avoid large deficit gaps.
