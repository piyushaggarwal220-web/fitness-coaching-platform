Amit, you've completed 4 weeks of Phase 1 with excellent adherence and strength gains. We're now progressing to a 4-day Upper/Lower/Upper/Lower split to increase frequency and volume while maintaining your 60-minute session target. This structure allows better recovery, more frequent stimulus on each muscle group, and aligns with your goal to reach 68kg. We're increasing overall volume by 15% through additional sets on compound lifts and strategic exercise additions.

Day 1 – Upper A (Horizontal Press Focus):
- Barbell bench press: 4×8
- Dumbbell rows: 4×8
- Incline dumbbell press: 3×10
- Cable lateral raise: 3×12
- Barbell curl (or cable curl): 2×10
Rest 90–120 seconds on compounds, 60 seconds on accessories.

Day 2 – Lower A (Squat Focus):
- Barbell back squat: 4×8
- Leg press: 3×10
- Romanian deadlift: 3×10
- Leg curl: 3×12
- Calf raise (machine or barbell): 2×15
Rest 2 minutes on squat, 90 seconds on leg press, 60 seconds on accessories.

Day 3 – Upper B (Vertical Pull Focus):
- Barbell rows: 4×8
- Dumbbell bench press: 4×8
- Lat pulldown (or assisted pull-ups): 3×10
- Machine chest fly: 3×12
- Face pulls: 3×15
Rest 90–120 seconds on compounds, 60 seconds on accessories.

Day 4 – Lower B (Deadlift & Quad Focus):
- Barbell deadlift (or trap bar): 3×6
- Goblet squat: 3×12
- Leg extension: 3×12
- Walking lunges (dumbbell): 3×10 per leg
- Leg curl: 2×12
Rest 2–2.5 minutes on deadlift, 90 seconds on goblet squat, 60 seconds on accessories.

Cardio: Maintain 3×30 min brisk walks on non-training days or post-session (optional, 10–15 min if added post-workout).

Progression cues: Aim to add 2.5kg to main lifts each week. If you hit all reps cleanly, increase weight next session. Maintain strict form—no ego lifting.
