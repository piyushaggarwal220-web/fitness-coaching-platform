**EATING OUT? USE THIS STRATEGY:**

When you eat out, make it count:

• Choose: Paneer tikka, grilled paneer, dal tadka, sabzi-roti combos
• Avoid: Fried starters, creamy gravies, naan-heavy meals, desserts
• Rule: One meal out = skip the evening snack that day to stay on track

---

**PROTEIN TARGET:**

You need ~90–100g protein daily for fat loss. Track roughly:

• Paneer (100g) = 18g
• Dal (1 cup) = 12g
• Curd (1 cup) = 6g
• Chana (30g) = 6g
• Protein powder (1 scoop) = 24g

If you're falling short, add 1 scoop whey post-workout or with breakfast.
