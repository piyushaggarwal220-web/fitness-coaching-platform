Weekly Diet Plan: Hey Rahul,

Great to have you on board! You're looking to build muscle over the next 12 weeks while staying consistent with your hostel routine and 3 gym sessions a week — that's a solid plan. I've built your week around your existing eating times (breakfast at 8:30, lunch at 1:30, evening snack at 6:30, dinner at 9) and stuck with foods you actually enjoy: dal, paneer, idli, curd rice, and chana. **MONDAY**

**Breakfast (8:30 am)**
Have 2 cups poha (approx 150g) mixed with 1 small onion, a pinch of turmeric, and a squeeze of lemon, served with 1 glass milk (200ml). Or swap the poha for 3 idli with 2 tbsp sambar and a small bowl of coconut chutney if you're feeling it.
(P: 12g | C: 52g | F: 8g | ~320 kcal)

**Lunch (1:30 pm)**
1.5 cups cooked rice (approx 250g), 1 katori cooked dal (approx 150g), and a small bowl of mixed sabzi (carrot, peas, beans — about 100g). Drizzle a tsp of ghee over the rice.
(P: 18g | C: 68g | F: 9g | ~420 kcal)

**Evening Snack (6:30 pm)**
Tea with 1 cup roasted chana (approx 40g) mixed with a pinch of salt and chaat masala. This is your regular routine — stick with it.
(P: 10g | C: 22g | F: 4g | ~140 kcal)

**Dinner (9:00 pm)**
2 rotis (approx 60g), 100g paneer (either paneer bhurji or paneer pieces in a light tomato gravy), and a side salad with cucumber, tomato, and onion dressed with a squeeze of lemon and a pinch of salt.
(P: 20g | C: 38g | F: 14g | ~380 kcal)

**Quick Coaching Notes:**

- **Consistency is your superpower.** You mentioned struggling with it — so keep meals at the same times each day. Your hostel mess timings are already set; use that structure.
- **Protein every meal.** Dal, paneer, chana, curd, and eggs are your friends. They're cheap, filling, and they'll help you build muscle.
- **Hydration.** You're drinking 2L water — try to push that to 2.5–3L on gym days. It helps with recovery and keeps you sharp.
- **Flexibility.** If the mess serves something different one day, swap in a similar dal or sabzi. The goal is to hit protein and stay consistent, not to be rigid.
- **Post-workout tip:** On your 3 gym days, have a light snack within 30–45 minutes after training — a glass of milk with a banana, or some curd rice. Doesn't have to be fancy.
- **Chaat masala and lemon** are your free flavor boosters — use them generously on dal, rice, and salads. They add zero calories and make food taste way better.

You've got this. Stick to the plan, hit your lifts, and we'll check in next week. Let's go!
