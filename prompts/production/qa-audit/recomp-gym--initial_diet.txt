Weekly Diet Plan: Hey Neha!

So happy to get started with you on this recomp journey! I know consistency has been tricky in the past, so I've built this week's plan around your existing meal times and the foods you already enjoy — dal, paneer, idli, curd rice — all in there. The goal is to keep things simple and sustainable while slowly shifting your body composition toward that 62kg target. Let's make this week count!

---

**Day 1 (Monday)**

**Breakfast — 8:30 am**
Have 1 plate (approx 150g) vegetable poha made with 60g poha flakes, loaded with peas, carrots, and peanuts (about 15g peanuts), or swap it for 3 idlis (approx 180g total) with 2 tbsp coconut chutney and 1 tbsp sambar.
(P: 12g | C: 52g | F: 10g | ~340 kcal)

**Mid-Morning — 11:00 am**
1 small apple or orange with 10-12 roasted almonds.
(P: 4g | C: 20g | F: 7g | ~150 kcal)

**Lunch — 1:30 pm**
1 katori (approx 150g cooked) moong dal, 1 cup cooked rice (approx 150g), 1 small katori mixed veg sabzi (beans, carrots, capsicum — around 100g), and a small bowl of curd (80g). You can squeeze a bit of lemon on the dal for extra flavour.
(P: 18g | C: 68g | F: 8g | ~410 kcal)

**Evening Snack — 6:30 pm**
1 cup tea with 30g roasted chana (the way you already love it!).
(P: 7g | C: 16g | F: 2g | ~110 kcal)

**Dinner — 9:00 pm**
2 rotis (approx 60g total), 1 katori (approx 120g) paneer bhurji made with onions, tomatoes, and a pinch of turmeric, and a big bowl of cucumber-tomato salad with a squeeze of lemon.
(P: 22g | C: 48g | F: 14g | ~410 kcal)
