**DAY 1 (Monday)**

**Breakfast (8:30 am)**  
2 idlis (approx 120g total) with 1 tablespoon coconut chutney and 1 small katori sambar (150ml), OR swap for 1 bowl poha (150g cooked) made with peanuts (10g) and a squeeze of lemon.  
(P: 10g | C: 50g | F: 6g | ~290 kcal)

**Lunch (1:30 pm)**  
1 katori cooked rice (approx 150g), 1 katori dal (150g cooked moong or arhar), 1 small katori mixed veg sabzi (100g), and a small bowl of curd (100g). If you want more protein, swap half the rice for an extra katori of dal.  
(P: 18g | C: 70g | F: 8g | ~420 kcal)

**Evening Snack (6:30 pm)**  
Tea (no sugar or very little) with 30g roasted chana. Add a small apple (80g) if you're extra hungry before the gym.  
(P: 8g | C: 22g | F: 3g | ~145 kcal)

**Post-Workout (if training today, around 7:30-8 pm)**  
1 scoop whey protein (25g powder) mixed in 250ml water, OR if you don't use whey, have 1 glass chaas (200ml) with a small handful of roasted peanuts (15g).  
(P: 24g | C: 6g | F: 3g | ~150 kcal)

**Dinner (9:00 pm)**  
2 rotis (approx 60g total), 100g paneer bhurji or paneer tikka (dry, minimal oil), 1 small katori sabzi (80g — beans, capsicum, whatever's handy), and a side salad (cucumber, tomato, onion, lemon).  
(P: 28g | C: 45g | F: 18g | ~450 kcal)

**Before Bed (optional, if hungry)**  
1 small glass haldi milk (150ml, low-fat) — great for sleep and a tiny protein bump.  
(P: 5g | C: 9g | F: 2g | ~75 kcal)
