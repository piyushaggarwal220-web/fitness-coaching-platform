**DAILY CALORIE TARGET: ~1,850 kcal | PROTEIN TARGET: 135g**

---

**MEAL 1 — BREAKFAST (8:30 AM) — ~450 kcal**
- 60g oats (dry weight) cooked in water
- 200ml low-fat milk (or unsweetened soy milk)
- 1 scoop whey protein (25g protein) mixed in or on the side
- 1 small banana or 10 almonds
- Black coffee or green tea (no sugar)

**MEAL 2 — MID-MORNING SNACK (11:00 AM) — ~180 kcal**
- 150g Greek yogurt (unsweetened) OR 100g paneer (raw weight)
- 5-6 almonds

**MEAL 3 — LUNCH (1:30 PM) — ~550 kcal**
- 80g rice (cooked weight ~200g) OR 2 medium rotis
- 150g dal (moong/masoor/toor — measured cooked)
- 150g mixed vegetable sabzi (lauki, beans, capsicum, spinach — minimal oil)
- Small bowl of curd (100g)
- Side salad: cucumber, tomato, carrot (unlimited, no dressing)

**MEAL 4 — EVENING SNACK (6:30 PM) — ~200 kcal**
- Tea with 100ml milk (no sugar or use stevia)
- 30g roasted chana OR 1 small bowl of sprouts (moong) with lemon and chaat masala

**MEAL 5 — DINNER (9:00 PM) — ~470 kcal**
- 2 medium rotis (whole wheat)
- 100g paneer bhurji or tofu stir-fry (measured raw, cooked with minimal oil)
- 150g vegetable sabzi (different from lunch — try palak, bhindi, or baingan)
- Large bowl of salad with lemon (cucumber, tomato, onion, radish)
- Optional: 50g curd on the side
