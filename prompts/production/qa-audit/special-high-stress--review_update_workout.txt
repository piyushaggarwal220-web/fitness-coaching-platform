**Upper/Lower Split – 4 Days Per Week**

You've completed your foundation phase. We're now moving to an upper/lower split to train each muscle group twice per week, which will accelerate fat loss and strength gains. Volume is increased by 15% while keeping sessions around 60 minutes. Focus on controlled reps and progressive overload.

**Day 1 – Upper Body (Push Focus)**
- Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets x 10 reps
- Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets x 10 reps
- Cable Tricep Pushdown: 3 sets x 12 reps
- Dumbbell Lateral Raise: 3 sets x 15 reps
- Cable Chest Fly: 3 sets x 12 reps

**Day 2 – Lower Body (Quad Focus)**
- Barbell Back Squat: 4 sets x 10 reps
- Leg Press: 3 sets x 12 reps
- Walking Lunges (Dumbbell): 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
- Leg Extension (Cable or Machine): 3 sets x 15 reps
- Standing Calf Raise: 3 sets x 15 reps

**Day 3 – Upper Body (Pull Focus)**
- Barbell Bent-Over Row: 4 sets x 10 reps
- Lat Pulldown (Cable): 3 sets x 10 reps
- Seated Cable Row: 3 sets x 12 reps
- Dumbbell Bicep Curl: 3 sets x 12 reps
- Face Pulls (Cable): 3 sets x 15 reps

**Day 4 – Lower Body (Posterior Focus)**
- Romanian Deadlift (Barbell): 4 sets x 10 reps
- Leg Curl (Machine or Cable): 3 sets x 12 reps
- Bulgarian Split Squat (Dumbbell): 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
- Hip Thrust (Barbell): 3 sets x 12 reps
- Seated Calf Raise: 3 sets x 15 reps

**Rest Days:** 3 days per week. Use for active recovery (light walks) or complete rest.

**Progression:** Add 2.5 kg to barbell lifts or 1–2 reps when you complete all sets with good form. Track every session.

**Tempo:** 2 seconds down, 1 second pause, 1 second up. Control the weight — no bouncing.

**Rest Between Sets:** 90–120 seconds for compound lifts (squats, bench, rows, RDL), 60 seconds for isolation moves.

This split trains upper body twice and lower body twice per week, hitting all major muscle groups with increased volume. Pair this with your cardio and nutrition plan for optimal fat loss.
