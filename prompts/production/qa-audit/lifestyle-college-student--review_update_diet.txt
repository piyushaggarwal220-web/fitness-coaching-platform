**MONDAY**

Breakfast (8:30 AM): 2 cups poha + 1 cup low-fat curd + 1 tbsp roasted peanuts
- Keeps you full; adds protein and fibre

Mid-Morning (11:00 AM): 1 glass buttermilk + 2 tbsp roasted chana
- Prevents the energy dip

- Structured snack. No binge risk.

- Protein-rich, satisfying, light enough for sleep.
