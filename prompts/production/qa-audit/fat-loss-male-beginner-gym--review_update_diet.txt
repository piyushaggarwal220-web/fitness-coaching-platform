Weekly Diet Plan: **ARJUN – YOUR UPDATED DIET PLAN**

First, let's address the elephant in the room: a 9/10 hunger level and weekend snacking tells me we need to fix your meal structure NOW. **KEY CHANGES THIS WEEK:**
1. Higher protein and fibre at every meal to crush hunger
2. Structured weekend meals – no more winging it
3. Strategic snack timing to prevent evening binges
4. Slightly higher calories (2100 kcal) to improve adherence while still losing fat

**DAILY NUTRITION TARGETS:**
- Calories: ~2100 kcal
- Protein: 140-150g
- Carbs: 200-220g
- Fats: 55-65g
- Water: Minimum 3L daily (non-negotiable)

---

**MEAL 1 – BREAKFAST (8:30 AM)**
- 3 whole eggs (scrambled/boiled)
- 1 cup cooked oats with 1 scoop whey protein mixed in
- 1 small banana
- Black coffee/green tea

*Why: Protein + fibre combo to keep you full until lunch. The oats-whey mix is a game-changer for satiety.*

---

**MEAL 2 – LUNCH (1:30 PM)**
- 1.5 cups cooked white rice
- 1 cup dal (moong/masoor)
- 150g chicken breast (grilled/curry)
- 1 bowl mixed vegetable sabzi (lauki, beans, or spinach)
- 1 cup curd
- Small side salad (cucumber, tomato, onion)

*Why: Your usual structure but with more chicken and added curd for protein. Veggies add volume without calories.*

---

**MEAL 3 – EVENING SNACK (6:30 PM)**
- 1 cup tea (no sugar or use stevia)
- 40g roasted chana
- 1 apple or 1 orange

*Why: This snack was too light before. Added fruit for fibre and volume to bridge the gap to dinner.*

---

**MEAL 4 – DINNER (9:00 PM)**
- 3 medium rotis (whole wheat)
- 150g paneer bhurji or paneer tikka
- 1 large bowl salad (lettuce, cucumber, tomato, carrots) with lemon
- 1 bowl vegetable soup OR sautéed veggies (optional but recommended)

*Why: Paneer portion increased, salad portion DOUBLED. Soup/extra veggies = fullness insurance.*

---

**WEEKEND STRATEGY (CRITICAL):**

Your weekends are sabotaging you. Here's your non-negotiable weekend structure:

**Saturday & Sunday:**
- Follow the SAME meal plan as above
- ONE flexible meal: Saturday dinner OR Sunday lunch – you can have a moderate treat meal (dosa, biryani, pizza – but ONE plate, not unlimited)
- NO unplanned snacking. If hungry between meals: carrots, cucumber, or 1 fruit
- Drink 1 glass of water before every meal

**If cravings hit hard:**
- Have 1 cup black coffee or green tea
- Eat 100g low-fat Greek yogurt or hung curd
- Go for a 10-minute walk

---

**HYDRATION & HABITS:**
- 3 litres water daily (track it)
- 1 glass water immediately upon waking
- 1 glass water 20 minutes before each meal
- Herbal tea allowed anytime (no sugar)
