**A few practical tips as you go:**
- On training days, try to have your lunch or a snack with protein and carbs about 2–3 hours before your evening gym session — it'll fuel your workout better.
- Dal is your best friend here — it's cheap, fills you up, and the protein stacks fast. Rotate between moong, masoor, arhar, and chana to keep things interesting.
- When you cook paneer curries, use tomato-based gravies or yogurt-based ones to keep the fat in check while staying tasty.
- Fish twice a week (Tuesday and Thursday/Saturday) will give you lean protein and omega-3s without the budget hit of chicken.
- Roasted chana is your go-to snack — keep a jar handy, it's portable and protein-dense.
- If you have access to whey protein, use it post-workout on training days to hit your protein targets more easily. If not, the curd rice or an extra glass of milk will do the job.
- Drink your 2L of water daily, especially around your training sessions.

Stick to the portions, don't skip meals, and trust the process. You've got this, Rohan. Let's build some muscle over the next 12 weeks.
