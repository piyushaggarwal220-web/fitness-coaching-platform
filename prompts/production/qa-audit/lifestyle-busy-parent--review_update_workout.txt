**Upper/Lower Split – 3 Days Per Week**

You'll now train two body parts per session to increase training density while keeping sessions around 30 minutes. This split builds on your foundation work and adds variety to stimulate new adaptation.

**Day 1 – Upper Body (Push Focus)**
1. Dumbbell Bench Press – 4 sets x 8 reps
2. Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3 sets x 10 reps
3. Cable Tricep Pushdown – 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Dumbbell Lateral Raise – 3 sets x 12 reps

**Day 2 – Lower Body (Quad & Glute Focus)**
1. Barbell Back Squat – 4 sets x 8 reps
2. Dumbbell Walking Lunges – 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
3. Leg Press – 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Standing Calf Raise – 3 sets x 15 reps

**Day 3 – Upper Body (Pull Focus)**
1. Barbell Bent-Over Row – 4 sets x 8 reps
2. Cable Lat Pulldown – 3 sets x 10 reps
3. Dumbbell Bicep Curl – 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Face Pulls – 3 sets x 15 reps

**Rest 60–90 seconds between sets. Warm up with 5 minutes of light movement. If a session feels too long, drop one exercise or reduce sets by one across the board.**
