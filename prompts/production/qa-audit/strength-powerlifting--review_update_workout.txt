**Advanced Strength Program – Upper/Lower/Push/Pull Split**

You've completed a full month on the 2-body-part split with excellent adherence and progressive overload. We're now transitioning to a 3-body-part-per-day structure to increase training density while keeping session duration around 90 minutes. Volume is increased by approximately 15% through additional working sets on compound movements and strategic accessory work. This split will provide fresh stimulus while maintaining your main lift priorities.

**Training Schedule: 4 Days Per Week**

---

**Day 1: Chest / Back / Rear Delts**

1. Barbell Bench Press – 5 sets x 6 reps (primary strength)
2. Barbell Bent-Over Row – 5 sets x 6 reps (primary strength)
3. Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets x 10 reps
4. Cable Seated Row (wide grip) – 3 sets x 10 reps
5. Dumbbell Rear Delt Fly – 4 sets x 12 reps
6. Cable Face Pull – 3 sets x 15 reps

*Rest 2–3 minutes between compound sets, 90 seconds between accessory sets.*

---

**Day 2: Quads / Hamstrings / Calves**

1. Barbell Back Squat – 5 sets x 6 reps (primary strength)
2. Romanian Deadlift – 4 sets x 8 reps
3. Leg Press – 3 sets x 12 reps (controlled tempo)
4. Lying Leg Curl – 4 sets x 10 reps
5. Walking Dumbbell Lunges – 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
6. Standing Calf Raise – 4 sets x 15 reps

*Rest 2–3 minutes between squat and RDL sets, 90 seconds elsewhere.*

---

**Day 3: Shoulders / Triceps / Abs**

1. Barbell Overhead Press – 4 sets x 8 reps (primary strength)
2. Dumbbell Lateral Raise – 4 sets x 12 reps
3. Cable Upright Row – 3 sets x 10 reps
4. Close-Grip Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets x 8 reps
5. Cable Tricep Pushdown – 3 sets x 12 reps
6. Hanging Knee Raise – 3 sets x 15 reps
7. Cable Crunch – 3 sets x 20 reps

*Rest 2 minutes between overhead press sets, 90 seconds elsewhere.*

---

**Day 4: Back / Biceps / Glutes**

1. Conventional Deadlift – 4 sets x 5 reps (primary strength)
2. Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldown – 4 sets x 8 reps
3. Dumbbell Single-Arm Row – 3 sets x 10 reps per arm
4. Barbell Curl – 3 sets x 10 reps
5. Dumbbell Hammer Curl – 3 sets x 12 reps
6. Barbell Hip Thrust – 4 sets x 12 reps
7. Cable Pull-Through – 3 sets x 15 reps

*Rest 3 minutes between deadlift sets, 90 seconds elsewhere.*

---

**Progression Protocol:**
- Add 2.5 kg to main barbell lifts when all prescribed reps are completed with good form across all sets
- For accessory work, increase reps before adding weight (aim for top of range +2 reps, then add load)
- Track all working sets in your log

**Warm-Up (Each Session):**
- 5 minutes light cardio (bike or rower)
- Dynamic stretches for target muscle groups
- 2 warm-up sets ramping to working weight on first compound lift

**Estimated Session Duration:** 85–95 minutes including warm-up and rest periods.

Continue your 3x weekly 30-minute brisk walks on non-training days for cardiovascular health and recovery. Maintain excellent sleep hygiene (7–8 hours) and hydration (2L+ daily). Your adherence and performance have been outstanding—keep that momentum while allowing this new split to challenge your muscles with varied angles and volume distribution.
