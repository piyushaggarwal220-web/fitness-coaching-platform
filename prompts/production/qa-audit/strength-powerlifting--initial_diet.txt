**Quick tips as you go:**

- **Drink plenty of water** — aim for 3–4 litres on training days. It'll help with digestion and keep you full.
- **Timing matters:** Eat your bigger carb portions (rice, rotis) around your evening gym sessions — that fuel will power your lifts.
- **Dal is your friend:** It's cheap, packed with protein and fibre, and stretches the budget beautifully. Don't skip it.
- **Roasted chana in tea:** It's a cheap protein snack and keeps you from reaching for biscuits. Toast a batch on Sunday.
- **Salad every day:** Keeps you full, costs almost nothing, and aids digestion. Vary it — tomato, cucumber, onion, carrot, radish — whatever's seasonal.
- **Fish on Tuesdays and Fridays:** Rohu or tilapia are affordable and lean. Pan-fry or grill with lemon and turmeric; no fancy cooking needed.
- **Paneer:** Use it 2–3 times a week for variety and satiety. A little goes a long way in a curry or bhurji.
