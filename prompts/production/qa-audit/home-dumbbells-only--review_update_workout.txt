**Day 2: Lower Body + Glutes**

Cooldown: 3 min stretching

**Day 3: Full Body Compound + Conditioning**

- Dumbbell Row: 3 sets x 10 reps
- Dumbbell Overhead Press: 3 sets x 10 reps
- Brisk walk or light cardio: 5 min
Cooldown: 3 min stretching
