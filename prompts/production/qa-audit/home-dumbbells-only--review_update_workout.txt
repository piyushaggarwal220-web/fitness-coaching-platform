**Weekly Structure: 3 days, 45 minutes each**

**Day 1 — Upper Body Push & Pull**
- Dumbbell Bench Press: 4×8
- Dumbbell Row: 4×8
- Lateral Raise: 3×12
- Dumbbell Pullovers: 3×10

**Day 2 — Lower Body Strength & Accessory**
- Goblet Squat: 4×8 (increased from 3×12 — your favorite, now heavier focus)
- Romanian Deadlift: 3×10
- Dumbbell Leg Curl (or Hamstring focus): 3×12
- Dumbbell Calf Raise: 3×12

**Day 3 — Full Body Compound & Core**
- Dumbbell Push-ups: 3×AMRAP
- Dumbbell Front Squat: 3×10 (variation from goblet)
- Dumbbell Rows: 3×8
- Plank Hold or Dead Bug: 3×20–30 sec

**Notes:**

- Maintain controlled tempo; no burpees as per your preference.
- If dumbbells feel light on Day 1 rows or squats, increase load by 1–2 kg next session.
- Total session time stays ~45 min with slightly increased density.

Perfect adherence & strong performance. Volume maintained, intensity increased via load & exercise variation. Monitor energy & hunger; adjust if needed.
