**UPPER/LOWER SPLIT — 4 DAYS PER WEEK**

You've completed your foundation phase. We're now moving to an upper/lower split to increase training frequency per muscle group while keeping session length similar. Volume is increased by ~15% through added sets. Given your low energy and sleep debt, we're using moderate intensity and prioritizing compound movements for efficiency.

**STRUCTURE:**
- Day 1: Upper A
- Day 2: Lower A
- Day 3: Upper B
- Day 4: Lower B

**REST:** 90–120 seconds between compound sets, 60–90 seconds for accessory work.

**PROGRESSION:** Add 1–2 reps or 2.5 kg when you hit the top of the rep range for all sets with good form.

---

**DAY 1 — UPPER A (PUSH FOCUS)**

1. **Barbell Bench Press** — 4 sets x 6–8 reps
2. **Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press** — 3 sets x 8–10 reps
3. **Cable Chest Fly** — 3 sets x 10–12 reps
4. **Dumbbell Lateral Raise** — 3 sets x 12–15 reps
5. **Overhead Tricep Extension (Cable or Dumbbell)** — 3 sets x 10–12 reps

---

**DAY 2 — LOWER A (QUAD FOCUS)**

1. **Barbell Back Squat** — 4 sets x 6–8 reps
2. **Leg Press** — 3 sets x 10–12 reps
3. **Walking Dumbbell Lunges** — 3 sets x 8–10 reps per leg
4. **Leg Curl (Machine)** — 3 sets x 10–12 reps
5. **Standing Calf Raise** — 3 sets x 12–15 reps

---

**DAY 3 — UPPER B (PULL FOCUS)**

1. **Barbell Bent-Over Row** — 4 sets x 6–8 reps
2. **Lat Pulldown (Cable)** — 3 sets x 8–10 reps
3. **Seated Cable Row** — 3 sets x 10–12 reps
4. **Dumbbell Bicep Curl** — 3 sets x 10–12 reps
5. **Face Pulls (Cable)** — 3 sets x 12–15 reps

---

**DAY 4 — LOWER B (POSTERIOR FOCUS)**

1. **Romanian Deadlift (Barbell)** — 4 sets x 6–8 reps
2. **Bulgarian Split Squat (Dumbbells)** — 3 sets x 8–10 reps per leg
3. **Leg Curl (Machine)** — 3 sets x 10–12 reps
4. **Goblet Squat** — 3 sets x 12–15 reps
5. **Seated Calf Raise** — 3 sets x 12–15 reps

---

**WARM-UP (5–7 minutes before each session):**
- 3 min light cardio (treadmill walk or bike)
- 10 bodyweight squats
- 10 arm circles each direction
- 5 inchworms
- 2 light sets of first exercise (50% working weight)

**COOL-DOWN (3–5 minutes):**
- Static stretches for worked muscles: 20–30 sec each
- Focus on hips, hamstrings, chest, lats

---

**NOTES:**
- Estimated session time: 55–65 minutes including warm-up/cool-down.
- If energy is very low on a given day, drop the last accessory exercise or reduce sets by 1.
- Prioritize sleep hygiene: aim for 7 hours minimum. Consider moving one workout to morning if evening fatigue is chronic.
- Track your lifts in a notebook or app to ensure progressive overload.
