**Upper/Lower Split – 4 Days Per Week**

You've been following a 3-day full-body foundation plan. We're now shifting to an upper/lower split across 4 days to increase training frequency per muscle group while keeping sessions ~60 minutes. Volume is maintained but exercises are rotated to provide new stimulus.

**Day 1 – Upper A (Horizontal Focus)**
1. Incline Dumbbell Press – 4 sets x 8 reps
2. Chest-Supported Dumbbell Row – 4 sets x 8 reps
3. Dumbbell Shoulder Press (neutral grip, elbows at 45°) – 3 sets x 10 reps
4. Cable Face Pull – 3 sets x 12 reps
5. Dumbbell Bicep Curl – 3 sets x 10 reps
6. Overhead Tricep Extension (cable or dumbbell) – 3 sets x 10 reps

**Day 2 – Lower A (Quad Focus)**
1. Goblet Squat – 4 sets x 10 reps
2. Leg Press – 4 sets x 12 reps
3. Walking Lunges (bodyweight or light dumbbells) – 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
4. Leg Curl (lying or seated) – 3 sets x 12 reps
5. Standing Calf Raise – 3 sets x 15 reps
6. Plank – 3 sets x 30–45 sec

**Day 3 – Upper B (Vertical Focus)**
1. Flat Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets x 8 reps
2. Lat Pulldown (neutral or underhand grip) – 4 sets x 10 reps
3. Dumbbell Lateral Raise – 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Cable Chest Fly – 3 sets x 12 reps
5. Hammer Curl – 3 sets x 10 reps
6. Cable Tricep Pushdown – 3 sets x 12 reps

**Day 4 – Lower B (Hip/Hamstring Focus)**
1. Romanian Deadlift (barbell or dumbbells) – 4 sets x 8 reps
2. Bulgarian Split Squat – 3 sets x 8 reps per leg
3. Leg Press (feet high and wide) – 3 sets x 12 reps
4. Seated Leg Curl – 3 sets x 12 reps
5. Seated Calf Raise – 3 sets x 15 reps
6. Dead Bug – 3 sets x 10 reps per side

**Shoulder Safety Notes:**
- All pressing is kept at or below shoulder height with neutral or 45° elbow angles to avoid impingement.
- No overhead barbell work or behind-neck movements.
- If any exercise causes shoulder discomfort, substitute with a pain-free variation or skip it.

**Progression:**
- Aim to add 1–2 reps or 2.5–5 kg when you hit the top of the rep range with good form.
- Rest 90–120 seconds between sets for main lifts, 60–90 seconds for accessories.

**Weekly Schedule Example:**
- Monday: Upper A
- Tuesday: Lower A
- Thursday: Upper B
- Friday: Lower B
- Adjust days to fit your evening availability; maintain at least one rest day between same-muscle sessions.
