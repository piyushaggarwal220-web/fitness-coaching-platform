Weekly Diet Plan: Hey Pooja!

So excited to get you started on this plan! Your goal is recomp — which means we're going to fuel your workouts well, keep protein steady, and create just enough of a deficit to nudge fat loss while protecting muscle. I've kept your meal timings exactly as you mentioned, and built everything around the foods you already love — dal, paneer, idli, curd rice — so this feels easy and sustainable. Let's make this week count!

---

**DAY 1**

**Breakfast (8:30 am)**
Have 1 plate idli (3 medium idlis, approx 150g) with 2 tbsp coconut chutney and 1 small bowl sambar (about 100ml). Or go for a bowl of poha (roughly 60g raw poha, cooked with peas, peanuts, and a squeeze of lemon) — both are light and energizing.
(P: 12g | C: 52g | F: 8g | ~320 kcal)

**Lunch (1:30 pm)**
1 katori cooked rice (approx 120g cooked), 1 katori dal (moong or masoor, about 150g cooked), 1 small katori sabzi (bhindi or lauki, around 100g), and a side of curd (100g). If you want more protein, swap half the rice for 80g paneer added to the sabzi.
(P: 18g | C: 68g | F: 10g | ~430 kcal)

**Evening Snack (6:30 pm)**
Your usual tea with a small katori roasted chana (about 40g). Keeps you full till dinner and adds a nice protein hit.
(P: 9g | C: 24g | F: 3g | ~155 kcal)

**Dinner (9:00 pm)**
2 medium rotis (approx 60g total) with 1 katori paneer bhurji (100g paneer scrambled with onion, tomato, and spices) and a big bowl of cucumber-tomato salad with a squeeze of lemon. The paneer will keep you satisfied through the night.
(P: 28g | C: 45g | F: 18g | ~460 kcal)

**Total for Day 1: ~1365 kcal | P: 67g | C: 189g | F: 39g**
