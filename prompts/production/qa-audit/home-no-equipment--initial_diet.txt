Weekly Diet Plan: Hey Tanvi!

Great to have you on board. I've built this plan around your current eating schedule — breakfast at 8:30, lunch at 1:30, evening tea around 6:30, and dinner at 9 — so it fits naturally into your day without asking you to overhaul everything. Since you're aiming for fat loss and want to hit 62 kg in 12 weeks, we're working with a moderate calorie deficit that keeps you energized for your evening workouts and doesn't mess with your sleep. The focus is on keeping protein solid (around 110g daily) so you hold onto muscle while we lean out, and I've stuck to foods you actually enjoy — dal, paneer, idli, curd rice — because consistency beats perfection every time. Let's go.

---

**MONDAY**

*Breakfast (8:30 am):* Have a bowl of poha (approx 150g cooked) made with 1 medium onion, 1 tsp oil, a pinch of turmeric, and finish with a squeeze of lemon and a small handful of roasted peanuts (approx 20g). Pair with a small glass of milk (200ml). If you're not feeling poha, swap it for 2 idlis (approx 100g) with a small bowl of sambar (approx 100ml) instead.
(P: 12g | C: 48g | F: 8g | ~310 kcal)

*Mid-morning (10:30 am):* A medium banana (approx 100g) or a small handful of roasted chana (approx 30g).
(P: 4g | C: 22g | F: 1g | ~100 kcal)

*Lunch (1:30 pm):* 1.5 katori of cooked white rice (approx 180g), 1 katori of dal (approx 150g) — moong or masoor works great — and a small bowl of mixed sabzi curry (approx 120g, any seasonal veg like beans, carrot, peas cooked in a light tomato-onion base with minimal oil). Finish with a small glass of buttermilk (approx 150ml) or plain curd.
(P: 38g | C: 68g | F: 8g | ~520 kcal)

*Evening snack (6:30 pm):* Your usual tea (approx 200ml, made with milk and 1 tsp sugar) with a small bowl of roasted chana (approx 40g) or a small handful of peanuts (approx 25g).
(P: 18g | C: 22g | F: 12g | ~240 kcal)

*Dinner (9:00 pm):* 2 medium rotis (approx 60g), 100g paneer bhurji (paneer crumbled and cooked with onion, tomato, and a pinch of turmeric in 1 tsp oil), and a side salad with cucumber, tomato, and lettuce (approx 150g) dressed with a tiny squeeze of lemon.
(P: 38g | C: 50g | F: 23g | ~580 kcal)

**Daily total: ~1750 kcal | P: 110g | C: 210g | F: 52g**

**A few practical pointers:**

— Keep your portions of oil/ghee tight — a teaspoon (approx 5g) per meal for cooking is plenty. A little goes a long way with flavor, and it keeps calories in check.
— Drink water before meals; it helps with satiety and keeps you from overeating.
— On your training days (I'm guessing evenings), have your evening snack about 30–45 minutes before you start, so you've got fuel in the tank.
— Curd is your friend — it's cheap, filling, and packed with protein. Don't shy away from it.
— If you're ever stuck without one of these veggies, just swap in whatever's available at your local market. The structure stays the same.
